    VEE'S PIERCINGS

    Teen Mom 2 star Vee revealed she's had her nipple pierced during a recent episode of her podcast Baby Mamas No Drama.

    While talking to co-host Kailyn Lowry, Vee shared spoke about her “crazy piercings” that include nipple and lip accessories.

    Intrigued by the news of the nipple ring, Kailyn cut her off and asked if she only has one nipple pierced.

    Vee bluntly replied: "Yeah, just one for fun."

    "You know how petty I am? I got it when me and Jo broke up," she explained referencing the pair's previous split.

    Her co-host loved the bold move replying: "Because you were like... having fun."

    The mom of one then continued: "I broke up with him and then I got my nipple pierced. And then I called him and showed him."

    CHELSEA'S PIERCINGS

    Teen Mom 2 alum Chelsea Houska, 30, also has a number of wild piercings.

    While the mother of four has her ears and nose pierced, her most daring accessory is likely her tongue ring.

    Chelsea - who has been known to experiment with her looks- got her tongue pierced back in 2011.

    She debuted the piercing with a selfie on Twitter, while she also took the opportunity to show off her newly dyed blond hair in the photo.

    According to Starcasm, Chelsea explained her decision to get her tongue pierced during an exchange with a fan on Twitter.

    After the fan told her she "did not have to" get her tongue pierced, Chelsea replied: "I randomly decided to do it today I had it pierced and wanted it again."

    Chelsea doesn't appear to rock the accessory anymore, though it was heavily featured on her Instagram account and during her time on Teen Mom 2.

    JENELLE'S LIP PIERCING

    MTV fans got a good look at Jenelle Evan's lip piercing while she was part of the Teen Mom franchise.

    Back in February 2021, the 30-year-old revealed she got her signature facial scar when she pierced her upper lip with a metal dart as a teenager.

    She shared the revelation on her TikTok and explained that she pierced her own lip at home before she got pregnant with her eldest son, Jace, 12.

    Jenelle began: "So when I was younger before I had Jace, before I was pregnant, before I was even on TV, I ended up piercing my lip by myself with a dart.

    "This was one of those like real metal, old-fashioned throwing darts that had the tip on it like [holds her fingers an inch apart] that big."

    The TV personality admitted she was forced to remove the piercing 12 years ago after it damaged her teeth, while she's been left with a scar ever since.

    "I took it out because it was rubbing the enamel off my teeth right there
    the metal part," she shared.

    "And what's crazy is that I've had it out since after I was pregnant with Jace, and the hole is still there."
