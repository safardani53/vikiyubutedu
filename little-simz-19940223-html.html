    Simbiatu 'Simbi' Abisola Abiola Ajikawo, known as Little Simz, is a Nigerian-born British rapper, singer and actor. After releasing four mixtapes and five EPs, she released her debut album A Curious Tale of Trials + People on September 18, 2015 through independent agency AGE: 101 Music. The album entered the UK R&B Album Chart at No. 20 and the UK Independent Album Chart at No. 43. She released her second studio album Stillness in Wonderland in 2016. Her third studio album Gray Area won Best Album at the Ivor Novello Awards and NME Awards and was nominated for the Mercury Awards.
