    It’s been roughly one month since we learned that Kylie Jenner and Tyga broke up.

    Since then, it didn’t take long for both parties to be linked romantically to other new boos.

    Just days after we learned of the breakup, Kylie was reportedly seen kissing rapper Ty Dolla Sign at a club, but shortly thereafter was seen on numerous occasions with another rapper, PartyNextDoor.

    In a few short weeks, Kylie and PartyNextDoor have already gotten serious, and she’s even allowed him to post her pics on Snapchat and Instagram.

    By contrast, Tyga was spotted at a concert with Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta star Karlie Redd and has also been linked to British model Demi Rose.

    While we haven’t heard much about Tyga and either of these women of late, the rapper just posted a new sultry pic of a new lady to Instagram.

    Her name is Amina Blue, and she’s – prepare yourself for a shocker – a model.

    But this isn’t just any model, it turns out. She’s a Yeezy model – that’s right, Blue is a fashion muse for Kylie’s brother-in-law, Kanye West.

    Would it hurt these people to date outside their inner circle? Sheesh.

    Anyway, Tyga was on a Jamaican vacay when he shared a pic of Blue looking camera-ready in a plunging silk blouse and bikini bottoms.

    Is he trying to make Kylie jealous? Or are things actually serious enough between these two to be Instagram official?

    Whatever the case, let’s get to know this mystery lady.
