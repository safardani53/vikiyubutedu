    Cow talked about his mom

    During last night's episode, Cow opened up about his mom in his clue package.

    “My mom is the strongest person I've ever met,” Cow said.

    “She’s been my foundation forever and she still is. Growing up I recall my mom having like seven jobs at one time.”

    Cow continued: “It was always my dream to find a way to take care of her in the way she took care of me.

    “The day I was able to support her with my music was the day I felt like I really made it."

    Cuddle Monster's main problem was the sweat

    NBA legend Metta World Peace, who was eliminated from the show two weeks ago, told Hollywood Life that his Cuddle Monster costume was heavy – and he sweat a ton under it.

    “There was just so much sweat,” he told the outlet.

    “I wish I would have had more towels.

    “But it was worth all the sweat,” he added.

    Donut shared heartbreaking message

    When he last competed on the Masked Singer stage, Donut opened up in a heartbreaking moment during his clue package when he spoke about his late wife.

    “I still can’t believe she’s gone. Most days, I just feel lost,” the singer said.

    “But I try to honor her memory every day by doing something brave.

    “It’s the whole reason I’m here, and it makes me smile knowing just how much she would love it.

    He added, “By the way, that’s just what I call her — my smile.”

    Donut will return for the Masked Singer Season 10 finale.
