    VALERIA Duque found herself in the middle of controversy when her name was linked to the breakup of Rosalia and Rauw Alejandro.

    Now, fans want to know more about the model.

    Who is Valeria Duque?

    Born on February 22, 1992, in Medellin, Colombia, Valeria Duque is a Colombian model, entrepreneur, and fitness and lifestyle content creator.

    She rose to fame when she starred in the reality competition series, Desafío Súper Humanos 2018.

    She has also starred in various music videos, including Maluma's 11 PM, Arnau's Escandalo, and Kevin Roldan's Medellin.

    In 2020, she launched a sportswear brand called Val Active with her friend Valentina Ospina, but it looks like it's no longer in business.

    She also noted on her Instagram bio that she's affiliated with Happily Gummies, a brand that offers sugar-free gummy supplements.

    How many followers does Valeria Duque have?

    Valeria has 1.5million followers on Instagram.

    Her feed is mostly composed of outfit photos, travel posts, fitness videos, and sponsorship posts.

    The model also has a significant following on TikTok, clocking in at over 400,000 followers. She has amassed 1.6million likes across her posts.

    She usually posts workout videos and TikTok challenges.

    Following Rosalia's split from Rauw Alejandro, Valeria made headlines when someone claimed that she was involved in the former couple's separation.

    She wasted no time to clear her name and shared a statement with her millions of followers.

    "Everything that is being said about me is FALSE, I have NO relationship with the person they are linking me to, I am the victim of FALSE accusations to create a media narrative that can destroy the reputations of various people," she wrote on her Instagram Story, which was written in Spanish.

    “I want to tell you that much of what you see on social media is LIES... I advise you to be more conscious when posting information that can unfairly affect people’s lives."

    She added: "I have been in this entertainment industry for a long time, I know many people in the field, and I have worked in it for years. I have been involved in important projects worldwide, and all the people who know me and the community that follows and supports me have witnessed the growth of my career and my work, in which my professionalism and discipline have characterized me."

    "THANK YOU to all who have sent me their positive energy, those who DO know me and know who I am, and there are MANY!!! With them, with the TRUTH and a clear conscience, I choose to stay."

    What did Rauw Alejandro say about the cheating allegations?

    Rauw also took to social media to shut down all the cheating rumors.

    In a message translated from Spanish, he penned: "Yes, a few months ago, Rosi and I ended our engagement."

    "There are thousands of problems that can cause a breakup but, in our case, it was not because of infidelity or a third person.

    "During this time that I’m taking to assimilate everything, there have been false public allegations, and because of the respect I have for her, our families and all we ever lived, I couldn’t stay quiet and continue to see how they try to destroy the most real love story God has ever allowed me to live.

    "With nothing more to add, to my fans that I love so much, thank you for being there.”

    Rosalia corroborated his statement, telling fans that she has much respect for her ex-fiance.
