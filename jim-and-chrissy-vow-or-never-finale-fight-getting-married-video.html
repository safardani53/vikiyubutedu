    To marry or not to marry, that’s the big question for Jim Jones and Chrissy Lampkin. In this EXCLUSIVE preview of the Oct. 6 finale, the couple clashes over their nuptials. Jim talks about pushing the wedding back, but Chrissy has something else in mind.

    “Maybe we just need to push it off the table altogether,” Chrissy tells Jim. She begs him to take marriage off the table for the sake of their relationship. “You’re not going to play with me. One week you want to get married, the next week you not,” she continues.

    Chrissy does not want to have to tell family and friends that they’re pushing the wedding back. Jim refuses to deal with the drama and tells Chrissy that she’s gone “off the deep end.”

    The end of the sneak peek features Chrissy making one shocking declaration: “I’m over it. No marriage. We don’t need it.” Sounds like the wedding is off!

    Elsewhere in the finale, Mama Jones meets with a psychic matchmaker and learns that the future of her son’s love life doesn’t look too bright. After Jim saw her naked in the house, Tiffany apologizes to Chrissy. Later, Jim and Chrissy call an unexpected dinner to announce that they’ve called off the wedding. Jim really wants the wedding to be back on and takes Chrissy on a last-minute yacht trip. Will it work? The season finale of Jim & Chrissy: Vow or Never airs Oct. 6 at 10 p.m. ET on WEtv.
