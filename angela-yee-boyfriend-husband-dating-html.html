    Angela Yee Bio: Ethnicity, Nationality, Parents

    Angela Yee was born on 3 January 1976 in Brooklyn, New York. She is of mixed Asian-black ethnicity; her father was a Chinese Cantonese and her mother a Caribbean Montserratian. Despite her mixed heritage, she belongs to American nationality.
