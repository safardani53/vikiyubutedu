    How old is Kelly Jones and who is his wife?

    Stereophonics frontman Kelly Jones is a 45-year-old singer, songwriter, guitarist, and director.

    Born June 3, 1974 in Cwmaman, Wales he formed the rock band with fellow Welsh musicians in 1992.

    He married his TV jounalist wife Jakki Healy in 2013 after first getting together in 2008.

    The pair wed in Co Clare, Ireland.

    PR guru Jakki has worked as journalist for MTV and together the couple share daughter called Riley, who they welcomed in March 2016.

    Kelly has two other children, Bootsy and Mitsy, 12, with his ex-girlfriend Rebecca Walters.

    Who are Stereophonics?

    Stereophonics are a Welsh rock band, which first formed in 1992.

    Kelly sings lead vocals and guitar, Richard Jones plays bass guitar, piano and backing vocals, Adam Zindani plays guitar and backing vocals, Jamie Morrison plats drums, and touring member Tony Kirkham plays keyboards.

    The group previously included Stuart Cable (1992 to 2003) and Javier Weyley (2004 - 2016) on drums.

    Their debut album Word Gets Around was released in 1997 and charted at number six in the UK.

    They finished a world tour in 2018 and released their 11th studio album, Kind, on October 25 2019.
