    Bones Season 10 Episode 10 was not your typical installment for two reasons:

  • It was the 200th (!) episode of the quirky FOX crime drama
  • It re-imagined the Jeffersonian teams in 1950’s Hollywood

    • Obviously, right?

    Watch Bones Season 10 Episode 10 Online

    Brennan portrays an LAPD detective, and Booth the robber to her cop, a jewel thief who is far more than meets the eye. It was an interesting concept.

    Brennan tracks Booth as he steals from a rich woman named Eva Bragga, who employs Cam as a maid in her house on Bones Season 10 Episode 10.

    Eva’s dead body is found, and Booth leaves without any jewels. He is now believed to be the killer, and it is up to Brennan to catch her man … or clear him.

     

    Brennan insists Booth isn’t the killer. Can she prove it?

    Hodgins, a paleontologist, is tasked with helping her examine the bones in the case. They visit Sarge, known in the present day as Angela’s father,

    Sarge insists that Booth and Brennan are made for each other. What do you know, eh?! Angela also them to date, and to find a man who can help him.

    Booth and Brennan visit James Aubrey at a club, where we also see glimpses of Daisy, Caroline, and a few other beloved figures from the 21st Century.

    Brennan realizes the killer is Cam, the maid, and she and Booth confront her just as she is trying to escape. Which she does, only to lock them in a closet.

    They kiss, but then Brennan is kidnapped, forcing hero Booth to pursue them and rescue his fair maiden just before Cam drops her into the sea from a plane.

    And with that, the 200th episode has come to an end.

    We’re guessing it’s back to 2014-15 next time around. Follow the link to watch Bones online and see this unique installment for yourself. It was certainly fun.
