    TWICE is a South Korean girl group that debuted in 2015.

    Given their soaring popularity, fans are curious to know who the members of the K-Pop group are dating.

    Who are the members of Twice dating?

    In Twice, the only members who are confirmed to have dated are Jihyo, the group's leader, and Momo, the main dancer.

    The other members of Twice are Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.

    In August 2019, it was reported that Jihyo was in a relationship with singer Kang Daniel. Per Soompi, both their agencies, JYP Entertainment and KONNECT Entertainment, confirmed the news, saying: "They are currently meeting with interest in each other."

    Meanwhile, in January 2020, The Korea Herald reported that Momo was dating Kim Heechul, a member of the boy band Super Junior.

    Heechul's agency, Label SJ, confirmed the report.

    "After getting along as close colleagues of show business, the two have recently started liking and dating each other," it noted in a statement.

    The news was controversial at the time, considering how Momo is 13 years Heechul's junior.

    When did Jihyo and Momo break up with their partners?

    Jihyo and Kang split in November 2020 after over a year of dating.

    JYP Entertainment confirmed it shortly after news of the breakup surfaced: "It is true that they broke up recently."

    According to South Korean media outlet Dispatch, the former lovebirds decided to split due to clashing schedules and a desire to prioritize work commitments.

    In July 2021, Momo and Heechul officially split.

    An unnamed source told South Korean news outlet Maeil Kyungje that the two have "drifted apart due to each of their busy schedules."

    Label SJ also confirmed the separation but didn't divulge further details.

    "After looking into the report, it is true that Heechul and Momo have ended their relationship. Please understand that it is difficult to say more as it is the artists' personal matter," they said.

    Are Twice allowed to date?

    All Twice members are permitted to date.

    Sana, one of Twice's sub-vocalists, told Radio Star that JYP Entertainment never imposed a dating ban on them, per Allkpop.

    However, the same can't be said for many K-Pop stars.

    CNN previously reported that many have a "no dating" clause in their contracts.

    What are the Twice members' current relationship status?

    Every member of Twice appears to be single at this time.

    In April 2023, they kickstarted their fifth world tour, Ready to Be, with stops all over the globe, including the US, Canada, Australia, Japan, and France.

    The tour includes two stadium shows: one at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles and MetLife Stadium in New Jersey.
