    MUSIC legend LL Cool J will host the Rock The Bells festival in Queens, New York in the Summer of 2022.

    The Forest Hills Stadium in Queens, New York, will host a lineup of hip-hop legends.

    Rock The Bells 2022: When is it and where can I buy tickets?

    The culture-rich festival will take center stage on August 6 at the Forest Hills Stadium in LL Cool J’s hometown of Queens in New York.

    In a statement, LL Cool J said, “This festival is my way of showing love to the community of hip-hop and celebrating the incredible journey this culture has taken, going from DJ Kool Herc’s Sedgwick Avenue rec room, to the global phenomenon we all cherish today.”

    Ticket pre-sale registration began on April 4 on the Rock the Bells festival site. General admission tickets sales begin on April 8.

    Who is in the lineup?

    The lineup boasts notable names and legendary hip-hop acts.

    Performances will include Ice Cube, Rick Ross, Fat Joe, Remy Ma, Lil’ Kim, Jadakiss, Trina, Digable Planets, N.O.R.E., and more.

    LL Cool J said it is an “honor” to bring these artists to the stage.

    More in music

    During an interview with Billboard, LL Cool J explained the need for the platform Rock the Bells is bringing to the festival circuit.

    “I found that so many people want this,” he said.

    “[Hip-hop fans] want to celebrate the culture, but they want it done in the right way.

    "So the same way that The Stones get treated, Paul McCartney gets treated, and Bob Dylan gets treated, I wanted to see these artists treated that way," he continued.

    The fest will be hosted by Roxanne Shanté with music by DJ Mister Cee and DJ Scratch.

    Why is the festival called Rock the Bells?

    The festival is named after the 1985 hit from LL Cool J.

    The upcoming Rock the Bells is different from a festival that ran from 2004 to 2013.
