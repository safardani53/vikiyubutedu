    Bath &amp; Body Works Candle Day 2022: When is it?

    AFTER a year of readjustment, Bath &amp; Body Works Candle Day 2022 is finally here and is going on right now. Shoppers will be able to splurge on candles to huddle around during the holidays. Heres everything you need to know about the long-awaited shopping day.

    22 Mar 2024, 00:00

    AFTER a year of readjustment, Bath & Body Works Candle Day 2022 is finally here and is going on right now.

    Shoppers will be able to splurge on candles to huddle around during the holidays. Here’s everything you need to know about the long-awaited shopping day.

    When is Candle Day 2022 at Bath & Body Works?

    The Bath & Body Works Candle Day sale in 2022 began on December 1 and will continue through December 2 and December 3.

    As reported by Style Caster, Loyalty Members were able to start shopping on December 1, while everyone else can begin on December 2.

    Bath & Body Works offers a number of deals throughout the year, but this is considered one of the most anticipated by shoppers.

    If you've ever purchased on Candle Day, you know that it used to be a one-day event in-store and online. However, in 2020, this was changed to a three-day event. 

    What can you look forward to on Candle Day 2022?

    To make your entire house smell holiday-ready, stock up on smells like Fresh Balsam, get extras of your favorite fragrances like Vanilla Bean Noel and Frosted Cranberry, or try one of the 40 new candles that will be released on Candle Day. 

    Most read in Money

    Most days of the year, Bath & Body Works' 3-wick candles cost $26.50, but on Candle Day, according to the company’s website, prices will be reduced by more than half. 

    Bath & Body Works’ popular three-wick candles are available for their lowest price of the year at $9.95, with 150 different variations to select from.

    There is a limit of 18 candles per client, though, so shoppers should choose wisely.

    Happy shoppers have taken to Twitter to express their thoughts on their purchases.

    On December 2, 2022, a user named Ashley tweeted:

    "In and out in half an hour. Got 11 candles, saved $180! Came home and placed an online order for 4 my store didn't have. Not a single issue using the app. I am extremely happy and satisfied with candleday2022!"

    A user named Khiem Tran said in a December 2, 2022 tweet: "And on this #CandleDay of 2022, the #BathAndBodyWorks #3WickCandle collection grows to 50! #CandleDay2022."

    Can you shop in store?

    According to Taste of Home, if shoppers can do so, shopping in-store is the ideal option for two reasons. 

    If shopping in person, according to the website, shoppers won't have to pay the $6.99 delivery fee that applies to internet orders. 

    And while online deals may last a day, in-store prices on Candle Day 2020 stayed the same for the whole three-day sale, while the online sale prices didn’t persist through day one.
