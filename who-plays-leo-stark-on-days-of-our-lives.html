    Who is Greg Rikaart?

    Greg Rikaart was born on February 26, 1977, in Brooklyn, New York.

    The 46-year-old actor has gained fame for his role as Leo Stark on Days of Our Lives and for playing Kevin Fisher on The Young and the Restless.

    According to his IMDb page, he has appeared in a number of other television shows, such as Days of Our Lives: Beyond Salem, Modern Family, Major Crimes, Bones, and CSI: Miami.

    As per his CBS Biography, Rikaart has received multiple awards, including a Daytime Emmy Award in 2005 and the Human Rights Campaign Visibility Award.

    He has been married to producer, writer, and actor Robert Sudduth since 2015.

    The pair share a young son named Montgomery.

    Rikaart is also popular on social media and has over 82K followers on his Instagram page, @gregrikaart.

    He joined the Days of Our Lives cast in 2018.

    Who is Leo Stark?

    Leo Stark is featured in both Days of Our Lives and Days of Our Lives: Beyond Salem.

    The character made his debut when Rikaart joined Days of Our Lives in 2018.

    According to the show's Wiki page, he is a con artist who lives in Salem, Illinois.

    The page's description of the character states, in part: "Leo was hired by Vivian Alamain to date Sonny Kiriakis, and then intern at TITAN where Sonny was CEO.

    "Not wanting to mix business with pleasure, Sonny broke up with Leo, but Vivian then ordered Leo to seduce Sonny as his employee, presumably create a scandal. Leo is found out by Kate, who enlists him to continue the takedown of TITAN after Vivian is killed."

    How can I watch Days of Our Lives?

    Days of Our Lives debuted on November 8, 1965, on NBC and is now available via Peacock.

    As of June 2023, there are 58 seasons of the hit TV series.

    Season 58 premiered on January 11, 2023.
