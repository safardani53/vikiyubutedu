    Who has Justin Timberlake dated?

    Prior to settling down and having a family with 7th Heaven actress, Jessica Biel, Justin Timberlake made headlines for more than just his music and film careers.

    Rising to fame in the late 1990s, the media has covered his career moves, while sleuths kept track of his romantic life.

    JT's first high profile relationship was with fellow singer, Britney Spears.

    The two performers met in 1993 while they both starred in The Mickey Mouse Club, years before their careers skyrocketed in the late 1990s.

    However, the two singers did not start dating until 1999.

    Spears and Timberlake called it quits in March of 2002, a break up aftermath that became center of media attention.

    After meeting each other at Nickelodeon's 16th Kids' Choice Awards in 2003, the Cry Me A River crooner and Cameron Diaz struck up a romance in April of that year.

    Following a December, 2006 episode of Saturday Night Live, where Diaz was the host and Timberlake was the musical guest, the pair were splitsville after the live taping.

    In 2017, Fergie confirmed to have a short fling with Timberlake in 1997.

    Other never confirmed, alleged flings include Emma Bunton, Beyonce, Scarlett Johansson, and Alyssa Milano.

    More on Justin TImberlake

    How long have Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel been married?

    Jessica Biel and Justin Timberlake first started dating in 2007.

    After four years together, the pair were splitsville in March of 2011.

    They came back from splitsville and got engaged in December of 2011.

    Their marriage ceremony took place at the Borgo Egnazia Resort in Italy on October 19, 2012.

    Jessica Biel and Justin Timberlake have two children together.

    What did Justin and Jessica say about his ex-girlfriend, Britney Spears?

    The former boy band member has received backlash after the release of the documentary, Framing Britney Spears.

    Fans have accused the singer and actor of making Britney look like a "villain" after they parted ways.

    Jessica Biel was more than supportive of her husband after he took to Instagram to open up and look back at past comments he made about his ex Britney after their relationship ended.
