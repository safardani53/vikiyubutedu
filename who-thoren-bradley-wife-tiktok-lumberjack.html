    Who is Thoren Bradley?

    Thoren Bradley is a health and fitness influencer who rose to fame after sharing videos to TikTok in which he chops wood with an axe.

    The outdoorsman resides in the woods of North California and has more than 9million followers on his page, bradley.thor.

    He is also popular on other social media apps, with 820K followers on his Instagram page, @bradley.thor; 534K subscribers to his YouTube channel, @ThorenBradley; over 10K followers on his X account, @ThorenBradley; and 10K followers on his Facebook page, @bradley.thor.

    The description on Thoren's YouTube channel states: "Evidence based fitness and health advice that you can easily apply everyday....while living a rural and outdoor friendly existence."

    Thoren is married to a woman named Angelique, who works as a realtor and interior designer in California for Keller Williams Realty.
