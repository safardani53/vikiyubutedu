    Popular discontinued breakfast favorite back 'by popular demand' - but there's a catch

    22 Apr 2024, 00:00

    A POPULAR cereal has been put back on the shelves after being discontinued, but not everyone can get their hands on it.

    Food manufacturer Malt-O-Meal has answered the cries of its Blueberry Muffin Toasters cereal fans by bringing the morning favorite, which was introduced in 2005, back to market.

    "We heard you," the company said in a news release last week.

    Malt-O-Meal announced that its "fan favorite" cereal is "back by popular demand," and it is "made with the same recipe as before" and on shelves at "select Walmart stores."

    The cherished cereal that's back and only sold in some Walmart locations "will be available in limited quantities," the food company added.

    "So make sure to grab a bag while supplies last!"

    More on cereal

    Fans of the cereal can use the store locator on Malt-O-Meal's website to find Blueberry Muffin Toasters cereal in nearby Walmart stores.

    "After the 2005 launch of Blueberry Muffin Tops cereal, the product then joined the Malt-O-Meal Toasters product family in 2013 and became Blueberry Toasters," Malt-O-Meal said.

    The manufacturer based in Northfield, Minnesota added that the cereal was reintroduced as Blueberry Muffin Toasters in 2020.

    The food company went on to say: "We are excited to re-introduce Malt-O-Meal Blueberry Muffin Toasters cereal in 2023 with vibrant new packaging!"

    Most read in Money

    The adored cereal is "a lightly puffed cereal made with whole wheat and rice, and features fresh-baked blueberry muffin flavor in every bite," Malt-O-Meal informed.

    Back in 2021, the food manufacturer broke the heart of one lover of the blueberry muffin cereal on Twitter.

    The fanatic asked if the Blueberry Toasters Cereal had "already" been discontinued.

    "After years of going without Blueberry Muffin Tops cereal and finally got a hint of its former beauty? I [can't] find it anywhere anymore," he added.

    "We are sorry to share that Blueberry Muffins Toasters has been discontinued," Malt-O-Meal replied from its verified account on the app.

    The tweet went on to say: "Generally our products are discontinued due to lack of demand or to make room for the introduction of new products,"

    "I will be sure to share your comments with our team."

    The Twitter user then replied to the company's account saying: "I promise it wasn't for lack of demand."

    "Everyone loves and misses the original Blueberry Muffins Tops. Just bring those back and you'll be golden," he added.

    Someone else said: "Bring them back. This was the # 1 best cereal, nothing else comes close."
