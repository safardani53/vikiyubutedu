    Matthew Wade Dillahunty is an American atheist activist who is currently president of the Austin Atheist Community, a position he held from 2006 to 2013. Since 2005, Dillahunty has hosted the webcast webcast The Atheist Experience. Previously he hosted a live radio program on the internet Non-Prophets Radio and created a project to challenge Iron Chariots. Dillahunty is a frequent guest speaker, or debate participant, for local lay organizations and academic groups under the World Student Union Speaker Office. In the summer of 2017, Dillahunty took a lecture tour sponsored by the Pangburn Philosophy Foundation, where he shared the stage with atheists Sam Harris, Richard Dawkins and Lawrence Krauss.
