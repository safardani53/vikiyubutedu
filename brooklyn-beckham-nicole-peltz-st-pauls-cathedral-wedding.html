    Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz in line to get married at St Paul's Cathedral

    BROOKLYN Beckham is in line to marry model Nicola Peltz in St Pauls Cathedral. Parents David, 45, and Victoria, 46, are entitled to enquire about the landmark as a potential venue for their sons nuptials because they are OBEs.

    27 Sep 2024, 00:00

    BROOKLYN Beckham is in line to marry model Nicola Peltz in St Paul’s Cathedral.

    Parents David, 45, and Victoria, 46, are entitled to enquire about the landmark as a potential venue for their son’s nuptials because they are OBEs.

    If Brooklyn, 21, and US actress Nicola, 25, were to choose St Paul’s — where Prince Charles and Diana Spencer married in 1981 — it would not be until 2022 at the earliest as there is a waiting list.

    The couple, who got engaged in July, are also expected to have a Jewish blessing in America to please her billionaire dad.

    A source said: “David and Victoria are aware Brooklyn can get married at St Paul’s. They are overseeing their British wedding and want to make sure it has the wow factor for Nicola’s family.

    “If they went ahead, the wedding would have real gravitas and set the benchmark for what comes later in America.

    “Brooklyn and Nicola are weighing up their options.

    “A wedding at St Paul’s has to include at least one Bible reading and contemporary music is banned so they would have to have something traditional.

    “Plus St Paul’s is open to the public so they would have a lot of people milling around. Also, there’s a long waiting list so it would be 2022 before they could go ahead, at the earliest.”

    Weddings at the cathedral take place in the Chapel of the Most Excellent Order of the British Empire in the crypt.

    Applications to marry there must be made through the Archbishop of Canterbury, who grants a special licence.

    It costs £3,240 for a ceremony with an organist, plus £325 for the licence.

    Musicians and recordings of the ceremony start from £1,000 each. The overall basic cost would exceed £5,000.

    Charles and Diana walked down the aisle at St Paul’s in front of 3,500 guests.

    It was an anomaly as royals usually get married at Westminster Abbey.

    In 2006, Jasper Carrott’s daughter Lucy Davis, 47 — who played receptionist Dawn in The Office — wed Welsh actor Owain Yeoman, 42, at the cathedral.

    Wheelchair basketballer and TV presenter Ade Adepitan, 47, tied the knot there with Linda Harrison in August, 2018.
