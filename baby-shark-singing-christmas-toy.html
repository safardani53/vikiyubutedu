    AS we're sure all parents will agree, it's impossible for our little ones to listen to the Baby Shark song just once.

    Well just when we thought our kids might be moving on from the strangely addictive jingle, singing Baby Shark cuddly toys have been released in time for Christmas - and it's threatening to kickstart the obsession all over again.

    Produced by the WowWee for PinkFong (Baby Shark's official creators), these plush toys sing the four-verse song in its entirety when you cuddle them.

    What's more, there's even a pink Mummy Shark and a blue Daddy Shark available to collect too.

    And while the soft toys would have set you back a staggering £62 last year, Amazon are currently flogging them for a much more purse-friendly £15.

    To avoid any Christmas Day disappointment, the toys also come with their own long-life batteries so your kids can start playing the tune on repeat right away... sure you're brave enough to do this?

    • Wow Wee Baby Shark Official Song Doll, £15 from Amazon - buy now

    What's more, the toys have racked up dozens of five-star reviews on Amazon - and much to our delight, mums have reported that the song actually plays fairly quietly.

    One wrote: "Amazon for kids that love the shark. Me personally, I can stand it and hide it at every opportunity I get."

    Another added: "My daughter loved this and the volume isn't as loud as the advert."
