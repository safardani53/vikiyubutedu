    CHANNEL 4's the Dog House has been warming our hearts since it first launched on 2019.

    But some fans of the canine show may be wondering where the hit series is actually filmed.

    Where is The Dog House filmed?

    The Dog House centres around the work of Wood Green, The Animals Charity, which is based in Cambridgeshire.

    The show is filmed entirely at the centre and follows the work of the dedicated staff members and vets who are committed to matching their abandoned dogs with loving owners for a forever home.

    The series shows the 60 strong team who work to counter the worst of our throw-away society, and witness stories of canine rescue and restoral.

    Wood Green takes in hundreds of disowned and neglected dogs every year.

    Although the show is based in Cambridgeshire, the team look for adopters from all across the UK.

    Can I visit the animal shelter?

    Visits to Woodgreen’s centre are by appointment only and are for people using their services, such as rehoming a pet or attending one of their workshops.

    You can find out more about making an appointment through their website.

    However, anyone is welcome to visit the charity shop at any time.

    You can support the good work at Wood Green by donating today.

    Just £5 a month could pay for a pet's essential first vaccinations before they're homed - you can even sponsor a dog for just 21p a day.

    You will receive a welcome pack, regular stories and updates about your pup.

    If you're looking for another way to help, you can also leave a gift in your will.

    The Animals Charity says gifts will fund more than half of everything they do.

    How can I apply to be on The Dog House show?

    The dogs are of course the main stars of the show, but Channel 4 is always looking for people to be on the series.

    If you think you can take in a rescue pooch on the series, you can apply here to be on the show.

    Their ultimate goal is to find each dog a forever home and a new human to love.

    How can I watch The Dog House?

    This tear-jerking show airs weekly on Channel 4 every Thursday at 8pm.
