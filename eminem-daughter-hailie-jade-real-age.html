    EMINEM fans are gobsmacked to learn that his daughter is now 28 years old.

    Hailie Jade, who was born on Christmas Day, celebrated her birthday early, posting a video of herself blowing out candles on a huge cake.

    She told her followers: “Early birthday celebration. I have a good feeling about 28.”

    Fans of the American rapper are very familiar with Hailie, as his hit singles are peppered with lyrics about his only daughter with Kimberly Scott.

    She featured in more than 20 of Eminem's tracks, including Hailie’s Song, Stan, Mockingbird, and When I’m Gone.

    Hailie’s Song was released in 2002 and fans can’t believe the little girl at the centre of it is now 28.

    A second said: “28!! I’m getting old ladies and gentlemen.. I remember her as a toddler like it was yesterday.”

    While a third added: “How is she 28? Where did time go?”

    Hailie graduated with honours from her school in Clinton, Michigan, in 2014.
