    THE New Year begins with tragedy in the Dales after a handful of teens decide to break the rules.

    Coming up in Emmerdale, Cathy Hope is on the verge of becoming a killer as she spirals out of control once again.

    While many are hoping for a peaceful and quiet start to 2024, the ITV Dales is a place of incessant drama, filled with devastating twists and blood-curdling turns - particularly if Cathy Hope (played by Gabrielle Dowling) is involved.

    This year, the teen caused trouble for herself and her family when she was revealed to be suffering from Premenstrual dysphoric disorder (PMDD).

    The condition, known as a very severe form of premenstrual syndrome, led her to have troubling outbursts, even against her own father Bob Hope (Tony Audenshaw).

    In scenes due on our screens, Cathy's behaviour takes another dangerous turn as she's keen to celebrate the New Year with her young friends.

    To make matters worse, Cathy drags her twin brother Heath (Sebastian Dowling) down with her as the pair come up with a plan to get to a party in Hotten for New Year's Eve - and behind their father's back.

    Later, while the adults are occupied at the B&B party, Cathy, Angelica King (Rebecca Bakes) and Heath take a joy ride in Wendy Posner’s car.

    Meanwhile, at the party, Nicola King (Nicola Wheeler) wants the kids to help serve and her obedient husband Jimmy (Nick Miles) is sent to find them.

    In the car, Angelica and Heath are flirting and they encourage Cathy to speed up.
