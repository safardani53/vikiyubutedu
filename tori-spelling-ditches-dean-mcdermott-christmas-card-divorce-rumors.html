    22 May 2024, 00:00

    TORI Spelling posed for a Christmas card with her five kids and several farm animals, leaving husband Dean McDermott in the dust amid reports they're divorcing.

    The card showed the Beverly Hills, 90210 alum, 48, holding a rooster alongside her children Liam, Stella, Hattie, Finn, and Beau.

    Also in the photo were two goats and a large pig.

    The card read: "Happy holidays. With love, from our farm to your home! xoxo, Tori & Family."

    Dean was not photographed nor was he mentioned on the card.

    Tori thanked the company "for once again bringing our happy family to life," calling the card her "favorite holiday card yet."

    Fans flocked to the card, for the most part ignoring Dean's absence.

    One fan did, however, ask: "Where's your husband."

    Another commented: "So she def split from Dean! Wow!" while another asked, "Are she and Dean separated?"

    Rather than speculate about the nature of Tori and Dean's relationship, other fans simple commented on how "cute" the photo was, or took the time to celebrate Tori and Dean's adorable family.

    "So cute! Love it!" one follower wrote.

    Another added: "You have a very beautiful family."

    The photo isn't the first Tori has excluded Dean, 55, from as the holidays approach.

    FESTIVE FAM

    Over the weekend, Tori and her kids posed for yet another festive photo, and once again there was no Dean in sight.

    Stella, 13, Hattie, 10, Finn, nine, and Beau, four, posed alongside Tori between two Christmas trees full of decorations.

    They were photographed standing beneath a large mistletoe and a sign that read: "Believe."

    The actress wished in her caption that the holiday season and all its cheer could be around "365 days a year."

    Liam, 14, was also not in the photo.

    Tori said she and the family had the "best time" together, adding that she got "so many beautiful vintage and modern decor pieces for the holidays" from the "family-owned business."

    She urged followers: "A MUST if you are in LA. And, they sell online too. This is in no way shape or form and ad. Just a huge new fan!"

    NO TORI

    Meanwhile Dean recently celebrated a birthday, leaving Tori out of his post.

    He showed fans the gifts his kids had gotten him, including a handmade work of art from Hattie.

    Dean pulled two canvases with horror-inspired art on them.

    He said in the clip: "It's my 55th birthday today and I am starting to open presents. Got one here from Hattie Cat for dad, and it says 'For dad. I love you so much. You are so kind. You are so loving and I am so happy to be your kid.. You are the best dad I could ask for."

    Dean also opened a card from his kids, however, it was not signed by their famous mother.

    Tori and the kids aren't just keeping Dean out of their Christmas photos.

    He was absent as the family celebrated Halloween together too.

    HALLOW-WHAT?

    Tori and the kids celebrated the spookiest night of the year with Pennywise the Dancing Clown on Instagram.

    The Scream 2 star was seen holding hands with the creepy clown while standing before a Mr. Floats truck.

    Earlier that month, Tori was seen leaving a meeting with her lawyer.

    She appeared to be having a serious phone conversation, shouting at some points.
