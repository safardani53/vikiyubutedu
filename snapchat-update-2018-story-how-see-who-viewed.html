    TRYING to find out who's been looking at your Snapchat Story? We've got an easy guide on how to do it.

    The latest Snapchat update has left everyone a little confused thanks to a redesign, but it's still possible to check out your Snapchat fans with the new app design.

    First, you'll need to make sure you have an active Snapchat Story. Snapchat Stories disappear after 24 hours, so double-check that you've actually posted something recently enough.

    Then, navigate to the launch page of your Snapchat app â€“ that'll be the screen with the camera.

    In the top left, you'll see a little profile icon. Tap it, and you'll be taken to your own profile page.

    On your profile page, you'll see a My Story section. You can click this and you'll be able to re-watch your own Story.

    If you swipe up from the bottom of the app, you'll see a view counter, which shows how many people have seen your Story.

    You'll also be presented with a list of people who have viewed

    On your profile page, you'll also be able to see a breakdown of individual Story posts that make up your overall Story.

    You can tap on these and then swipe up to see views for each specific photo or video you've uploaded.

    In any case, you're not alone in feeling a little confused by Snapchat's new redesign.

    It's proved so controversial that more than a million people have signed a petition pleading with Snapchat to roll back the update.

    The update made sweeping changes to the design of the Snapchat app, including merging the old 'Chats' and 'Stories' tabs into a single 'Friends' panel.

    But Snapchat isn't looking likely to revert to the old design, and hopes users will eventually get used to the new app.
