    Aaron Jones | Early Career

    Jones was a multi-sport star from his school days. He was active in football, track, and basketball. In school football, he played as a running back, wide receiver, and defender. He was also honored All-City and All-District honors for his remarkable performance.

    Aaron showed his amazing performance in his first years, which astonished everyone. However, he had a severe ankle injury in his junior year.

    After recovering from the injury, he returned stronger in his senior season. As a result, he broke several college records and won several honors and awards for his fantastic performance.

    As a runner, he holds the school’s records for running yards (4,114), season (1,773), and career yards per game (117.5).
