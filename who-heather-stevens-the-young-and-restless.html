    THE YOUNG and the Restless has been on the air for five decades.

    Now, fans want to know more about Heather Stevens from the hit TV show.

    Who is Heather on The Young and the Restless?

    As Showbiz CheatSheet notes, Heather Stevens is a legacy character on The Young and The Restless who disappeared from the series for ten years.

    Soap Opera Digest reported that actress Vail Bloom would return as Heather for a February 2023 "visit" to the show's fictional version of Genoa City, Wisconsin.

    CheatSheet states that Heather was born in 1979 and is the daughter of the legacy character Paul Williams and April Stevens.

    Following the pair's divorce, Heather moved to New York with April.

    According to the outlet, she returned to Genoa City in 2007 and worked on mending her relationship with Paul.

    She followed in her father's footsteps by beginning a law career and becoming the town's assistant district attorney.

    Heather's "quest for justice" lead to conflicts with other characters, including Phyllis Summers and Victor Newman.

    Throughout her time on the popular soap, Heather has become romantically involved with multiple characters, including Adam Newman, Ronan Malloy, Chance Chancellor, and Daniel Romalotti.

    As per CheatSheet, in 2012, she moved to Savannah, Georgia, with Daniel — the son of Phyllis — and his daughter, Lucy Romalotti.

    Daniel went home to Genoa City in November 2022 without Heather or his daughter.

    The outlet notes that Heather and Lucy's February 2023 return to the television series is due to Phyllis.

    It states: "Daniel admitted that he and Heather are having problems because of her new job in Portugal.

    "While Daniel’s busy reconnecting with his ex-wife Lily Winters (Christel Khalil), Phyllis has other plans.

    "Phyllis secretly goes to Portugal to bring Heather and Lucy to Genoa City."

    As per Soaps In Depth: "Heather showed up in February 2023 to talk with Daniel, who was happy to have the chance to show his ex how he was a changed man.

    "While Heather appreciated that Daniel had pulled himself out of the darkness, she also admitted it was too late because she had met someone else and fallen in love!

    "After ending things with Daniel, Heather returned to Portugal with Lucy."

    Heather once again returned to the show in July 2023 and delivered some news to Phyllis.

    As per the outlet: "Heather surprised Phyllis when she knocked on her door to announce that with Michael away on business, she would be representing her on the charges of faking her own death and killing Jeremy Stark in self-defense.

    "Although reluctant, Phyllis agreed."

    Who plays Heather on The Young and the Restless?

    Multiple actresses have played the role of Heather throughout the years.

    According to CheatSheet, various child actors played Heather during her early life.

    Teenaged Heather was portrayed by Conci Nelson, the daughter of the soap's former lead writer, Kay Alden.

    Vail began playing the character in July 2007 before leaving in March 2010.

    Former All My Children actress Eden Riegel then took on the role before she left in November 2011, as per CheatSheet.

    Jennifer Landon, who previously starred in As the World Turns, played Heather for four months, beginning in June 2012.

    As of 2023, Heather is once again portrayed by Vail.

    How can I watch The Young and the Restless?

    Fans can tune in to The Young and the Restless on CBS.

    It is also available via the CBS website, Pluto TV, and Paramount Plus.
