    JUDGING by their social media exchanges, things appeared to be friendly with Drake and Bobbi Althoff.

    However, rumors of a rift between the two started when the podcaster and the Certified Lover Boy rapper unfollowed each other on Instagram.

    What happened between Bobbi Althoff and Drake?

    On August 12, 2023, Bobbi Althoff attended Drake's concert at Kia Forum in Inglewood.

    Days later, Bobbi and Drake unfollowed each other's Instagram pages.

    The influencer also deleted her interview with the GRAMMY Award winner from her YouTube channel The Really Good Podcast.

    Before she deleted the podcast episode with the Her Loss rapper, the video already accumulated millions of views.

    As fans of both continue to speculate about what happened between the two, the guesses lie between a couple of theories.

    Online sleuths think Drake took offense to Bobbi's indifferent reaction to seeing him in concert; resulting in their fallout.

    On X, one fan remarked how Bobbi "looked like she was being held captive" and tweeted: "My [question mark emoji] to all of you this morning is why are we surprised, 1st it's their business right but why are we surprised at #Drake and #Bobbi...

    "She looked like she was being held captive at his concert last night [laughing emoji]."

    Another user chimed in: "When I go to concerts, let alone a Drake concert, (with my friends) I’m finding every possible way to turn up, even if I don’t know the song. This Bobbi chick’s lame."

    A fourth tweeted: "People really upset Bobbi didn't catch 'the vibe' at Drake's concert [laughing emoji]."

    Another group of sleuths have theorized Drake attempted to pursue a romantic relationship with Bobbi but the former's feelings weren't returned by the latter.

    Taking to Twitter, one observer speculated: "Drake Reportedly Tried To Sleep With Bobbi Athloff After She Attended His Concert But She Rejected Him.

    "The Interview Was Then Pulled & He Blocked Her On All Socials."

    Another fan said: "Bobbi did nothing wrong. Drake can’t be trusted."

    Meanwhile, an additional sleuth concluded: "Everyone thinks it’s Drake when in reality I think it was Bobbi’s husband who probably got salty and made her block him & repost him on her Instagram."

    "He had to have made a move on her or something," commented a fourth user.

    What did Bobbi say about the rumors?

    On August 16, 2023, Bobbi took to her Instagram story to lay the rumors to rest by sharing "an uncensored DM" exchange she’d had with Barstool Sports founder Dave Portnoy.

    “My girlfriend says you hooked up with Drake and got divorced. I am saying that is not true,” Dave's message read.

    Bobbi replied, “I am not commenting publicly but off the record, you’re right that is not true.”

    Atop the screenshot, she added: “I did not want to do this podcast in the first place, and now so much negativity is coming from it.”

    The 26-year-old vowed to “leave it alone after this” without elaborating on what she and the rapper are beefing about.

    How did Bobbi land her interview with Drake?

    Prior to her wiping it from her YouTube channel, Bobbi's interview with Drake was one of her highest-viewed videos.

    Regarding how the two came together, Bobbi revealed the hitmaker liked her episode with Funny Marco and followed her.

    After the rapper followed her, Bobbi took a chance and sent him a direct message.

    "I decided to just go for it and shoot my shot," the podcaster told Cosmopolitan.

    "I DMed Drake and asked if he wanted to be on my podcast, and he said yes.

    "He sent me his touring schedule, and I knew that I needed to make it happen fast, so my friend and I flew to Memphis two days after the original DM was sent to record the episode."

    Is Bobbi Althoff still married?

    Bobbi is married to tech executive, Cory Althoff.

    The pair reportedly got engaged back in October 2019.

    In an August 2023, interview with Cosmopolitan, Bobbi told the outlet: "I prefer to keep my relationship private."

    "I’m very insecure, and the character that I’ve created is made up of my biggest insecurities. I’ve just made her into someone who’s proud of who she is.”

    Nevertheless, Cory's LinkedIn reveals he works as the senior vice president of Software Development Programs at CompTIA - a company he first joined in April 2022.

    Bobbi and Cory share two children together and in her interview with Cosmopolitan, the influencer disclosed that she's wanted a be a mother her whole life.
