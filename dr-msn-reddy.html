    Dr. MSN Reddy Biography

    Dr. MSN was born on August 27, 1970. He was born and raised in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. He frequently travels to different countries to conduct research on medicines and remedies.

    Dr. MSN completed his senior secondary education at his hometown school. He enrolled at Osmania University in India to pursue a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Organic Chemistry. He began his career as a chemist and now owns his own drug manufacturing company.

    Dr. MSN Reddy hasn’t revealed anything about his parents or siblings. He devoted his entire life to his studies, and he is now a well-known Indian entrepreneur. According to Quora, Reddy is a member of one of the most powerful castes, so he practices Hinduism and belongs to the Kapu caste of the local chieftain class.

    Dr. MSN Reddy Wife & Marriage

    According to Deccan Chronicle, he lives with his wife, Prasuna, in a large bungalow in Jubilee Hills. His daughter is Sindhuja, and his son is Achyuth. The family shared a 17000 square foot villa.

    Dr. MSN Reddy Professional Career

    MSN Laboratories Pvt. Ltd. was founded by Dr. MSN. It began in 2003 in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This company considers itself to be the world’s most successful manufacturer and pharmaceutical R&D company. It provides patients with the option of home delivery at very reasonable prices.

    According to MSN Labs’ official website, it assisted over a thousand people by providing them with medications. Dr. MSN has also made a medical contribution to the pandemic. He has developed Oselow, an Oseltamivir 75 mg capsule that aids in the treatment of viral flues.

    Dr. MSN has developed a coronavirus treatment called Favipiravir (Favilow) 800mg tablet. It will be available in April 2021 for Rs. 144 per tablet. The drug was approved by India’s Drug Controller General (DCGI). It is a high-potency drug that aids in the fight against the virus.

    Prior to this medicine, in August 2020, they produced Favipiravir 200mg and 400mg, which were less potent. This medication holds the record for being the cheapest medication. It only costs Rs. 39 per tablet. Dr. MSN also provides medicines in other countries.
