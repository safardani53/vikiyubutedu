    EVERYONE knows that Mr Whippy is the king of all the ice-creams.

    The second you hear that ice-cream tune van playing down the road, it conjures up memories of tucking into a soft creamy delight on a crunchy cone.

    But one woman worked out how to recreate the much-loved summer treat and it costs less than £2.10.

    Using just two ingredients Emmah Ord showed how to make ice-cream that tastes "exactly the same".

    She shared a video on TikTok - which has already amassed more than 1.3 million views.

    In order to make the yummy sweet treat she mixed condensed milk and double cream.

    Condensed milk can be bought for £1.05 for a pot in Sainsbury's and double cream costs the same, making the total spend £2.10.

    Emmah said: "Rumour has it that you can make Mr Whippy ice cream using condensed milk and double cream - so obviously, I'm going to give this a go.
