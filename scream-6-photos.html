    Ghostface is back, and it’s a whole new ball game. The four survivors from ‘Scream 5’ have left Woodsboro behind to start anew in New York City. Unfortunately, a new Ghostface has followed them. Ghostface is ready to have a bloody good time in the Big Apple.

    Sam, Tara, Mindy, and Chad will cross paths with Gale Weathers again. They’ll also meet Kirby Reed, one of the survivors of the Woodsboro Massacre Remake of ‘Scream 4.’ This marks Hayden Panettiere’s first role since 2018. It’s good to have Kirby back.

    Melissa Barrera, Jasmin Savoy Brown, Mason Gooding, Jenna Ortega, Hayden Panettiere, and Courteney Cox return to their roles in the franchise alongside Jack Champion, Henry Czerny, Liana Liberato, Dermot Mulroney, Devyn Nekoda, Tony Revolori, Josh Segarra, and Samara Weaving. Neve Campbell will not be returning as Sidney Prescott in the film. ‘Scream 6’ hits theaters on March 10, 2023. See photos from the film in our gallery now.
