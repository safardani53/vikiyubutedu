    Win tickets to The Baby Show in Manchester

    THE Baby Show with Lidl GB, the UKs largest pregnancy and parenting event, is coming to Manchester Central from Friday, April 14 to Sunday, April 16 and we have partnered with The Baby Show to give away over 100 pairs of tickets.

    22 Jul 2024, 00:00

    THE Baby Show with Lidl GB, the UK’s largest pregnancy and parenting event, is coming to Manchester Central from Friday, April 14 to Sunday, April 16 and we have partnered with The Baby Show to give away over 100 pairs of tickets.

    Plus one lucky reader will win the grand prize of a baby bundle and tickets to the show, worth £275, from top exhibitor, Tommee Tippee.

    The event will give new and expectant parents across Manchester and the region, the chance to shop for everything they could need for their new arrival, all under one roof.

    Filled with amazing bargains and huge discounts from many of the 250 baby brands exhibiting, the show comes at the perfect time as visitors can save time and money, making their baby budget stretch even further. 

    Whether they’re buying their first ever buggy, car seat or cot, the show gives visitors the chance to compare, try, buy and save on products, as well as walk away with all the knowledge and expertise they can learn from the show experts. 

    One entrant will also take home the grand prize of a Tommee Tippee bundle.

    More on parenting

    This includes a complete feeding kit, a Closer to Nature Clear Baby Bottle Starter Set, a Twist & Click Nappy Disposal Bin, a Dreammaker Night Light & Baby Sleep Aid and Mini Grofriend Travel Sleep Aid – all the essentials as your start your journey as a new parent!
