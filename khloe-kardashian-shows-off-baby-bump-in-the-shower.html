    Khloe Kardashian Shows Off Baby Bump in the Shower

    About a month ago, Khloe Kardashian revealed her baby bump right on the heels of Kylies announcement that shed given birth. Now, however, Khloe is about 31 weeks along and her baby will be here before you know it.

    02 Jul 2024, 00:00

    About a month ago, Khloe Kardashian revealed her baby bump — right on the heels of Kylie’s announcement that she’d given birth.

    Now, however, Khloe is about 31 weeks along — and her baby will be here before you know it.

    She’s celebrating her impending motherhood with a sexy photoshoot — in the shower.

    If, like, a fortune-teller had told Khloe that she was fated to give birth, but only after her baby sister Kylie did, do you think that Khloe would have imagined that she’d become a mother at 33?

    (Or would she have started pushing teenage Kylie to get pregnant? No, no, let’s not let our imaginations run away with the possibilities)

    As it turned out, however, Khloe didn’t have to wait until her forties and Kylie apparently didn’t need any help getting knocked up as a teenager.

    And Khloe is pregnant with Tristan Thompson’s baby.

    Many are hopeful that Khloe, who’s always seemed eager to manage and meddle in the personal choices of her family members, will be mellowed out by motherhood.

    Khloe has debuted these new photos of herself, dressed in a sexy dress, cradling and flaunting her 8-month baby bump in the shower.

    Her caption on the photo: "What’s meant to be will always find its way."

    Why is she in the shower?

    Why does Khloe does any of the things that she does, honestly? It’s a cool spot for an unusual photo. Why not?

    (Maybe it’s a "baby shower" joke)

    Khloe’s gotten flack from haters for having the audacity to touch her own baby bump in photos, and clapped back pretty soundly, saying:

    "I choose to cradle my bump because it’s MINE. I’ve waited for this VERY short moment for YEARS."

    Honestly, she doesn’t need to defend touching her own abdomen, but she continues:

    "I have only months to enjoy this phase in my life, so I will touch my bump and love my bump as often as I choose. Mommy loves you baby!"

    (Yeah, this photo was a bit blurrier — but still great)

    Khloe Kardashian has shared a lot about her pregnancy experience.

    Arguably, she’s overshared a bit. Sort of odd for a woman who waited months before confirming her pregnancy.

    For one thing, she opened up in response to a fan question, and revealed that she has had sex while pregnant.

    Her takeaway was that pregnancy sex was doable but got awkward as things progressed.

    In part, physical body positions during pregnancy sometimes need to be different so that everyone is comfortable when there’s a huge baby bump in the way.

    At the same time, however, Khloe admitted to feeling self-conscious about her appearance — even though she insisted that baby daddy Tristan Thompson had never said anything to make her feel bad.

    Khloe officially announced her pregnancy in December, months after the entire world knew about it.

    At the time, she shared a photo of her baby bump, writing:

    "My greatest dream realized! We are having a baby! I had been waiting and wondering but God had a plan all along. He knew what He was doing. I simply had to trust in Him and be patient."

    After her religious thanks for this long-awaited news, Khloe thanked the guy who had a direct hand — among other body parts — in her pregnancy.

    "I still at times can’t believe that our love created life! Tristan, thank you for loving me the way that you do! Thank you for treating me like a Queen! Thank you for making me feel beautiful at all stages! Tristan, most of all, Thank you for making me a MOMMY!!!"

    That is so sweet, right?

    "You have made this experience even more magical than I could have envisioned! I will never forget how wonderful you’ve been to me during this time! Thank you for making me so happy my love!"

    Khloe also apologized to her fans at the time for having kept them in the dark — sort of — by not confirming her pregnancy.

    "Thank you to everyone for the love and positive vibes! I know we’ve been keeping this quiet but we wanted to enjoy this between our family and close friends as long as we could privately. To enjoy our first precious moments just us"

    That totally makes sense and is her right.

    Considering all of the photos that she’s shared since then, including this sultry shower shoot, she’s really making up for it. Don’t you agree?

    In the mean time, let’s all look forward to Khloe’s shocking gender reveal.
