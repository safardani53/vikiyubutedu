    Cristina Adriana Chiara Scabbia is an Italian singer and lyricist, best known as one of the two vocalists in the Italian gothic metal band Lacuna Coil. She used to write an advice column in the popular rock magazine Revolver, alongside musician Vinnie Paul. Scabbia is featured in a Megadeth song “À Tout le Monde (Set Me Free)”. Scabbia performed a duet with the Italian ex-singer of Linea 77 Emiliano Audisio on the track “Beautiful Lie” to be part of the soundtrack for the 2013 Italian film Passione sinistra.
