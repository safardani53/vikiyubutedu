    A BARGAIN hunter has found an extremely sought-after craft tool marked down by almost $250.

    A TikTok page named ExtremeWalmartClearance has lived up to its name with its most recent find.

    Earlier this week, the money-saving family headed to Walmart to search for a Cricut, which is a machine that precisely cuts out letters, shapes and more from various materials for arts and crafts.

    In the TikTok, the family headed to the crafts and sewing aisle to search through the locked displays of Cricuts and Cricut supplies.

    Most of the machines retail for upwards of $250, but the specific model they were searching for originally cost about $300.

    After scouring shelves and glass cases for the Cricut, they decided to ask for assistance and an employee was able to find it for them pretty quickly.

    Read more on Walmart

    When they took it to the register, it rang up for $54, despite the fact it wasn't in the usual clearance section.

    While you may want to rush to your nearest Walmart to try and snag one for yourself, it's important to remember that price and availability varies from store to store.

    HOW TO SAVE AT WALMART

    If you feel like you're missing out on all the incredible deals, we have some tips that could help you spot them.

    For starters, you should be aware of Walmart's "hidden clearance" items.

    These are products that have been marked down to clearance prices but not yet labeled as so.

    In order to find these, you should download the Walmart app and use the Scan & Go feature.

    Scan items you find on the sales floor to see how much it's going for at the specific location you're shopping in.

    Clearance items at Walmart can be marked down by as much as 90 percent, offering maximum savings.

    Shoppers have been able to get items for just a few cents under these sales.

    These sales are more common than you'd think.

    One shopper was able to save over $220 on a single item.
