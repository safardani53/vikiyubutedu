    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

    For many people who celebrate the holiday, the best part about Christmas is not always the gifts but the opportunity to decorate with Christmas trees, lights, music and more. Whether you're putting up your own Christmas decorations or traveling around town to take in the festive spirit in your community, it's undeniable how magical the holidays feel with the right trimmings.

    The Kardashian-Jenner family is known for going all out when Christmas time rolls around, with the decor spreading from room to room, lining the halls and even serving as a backdrop for the bathroom.

    Check out how the merry crew has decorated for the holidays throughout the years.

    2023: Kim Kardashian’s Dazzling Lights

    Kim Kardashian posted an Instagram Story showing bright white lights adorning the rows of tall trees in front of her Los Angeles home the Sunday after Thanksgiving.

    The SKIMS founder filmed the footage while pulling into her driveway, and set the video to Michael Bublé’s “It’s Beginning to Look a Lot Like Christmas."

    2023: Kourtney Kardashian’s Homemade Elf Display

    Kourtney Kardashian Barker declared “elf season has begun” while sharing a glimpse of her lit-up Christmas tree and a homemade display of four Elf on The Shelf dolls sledding.

    The elves' sleds were made of silver foil plates, while clusters of cotton balls represented snow. A sign reading “Elf Sledding Now Open” was handwritten and placed on the top of the elf display. 

    2023: Travis Barker’s Spooky Skeleton

    Kourtney’s husband Travis Barker took a note from Halloween when displaying his Christmas tree in 2023. In a photo Barker shared to his Instagram Story, a towering skeleton appears to help decorate the evergreen, holding a string of white lights.

    The Blink-182 drummer's display also features musical props, including a silver stereo and a red-and-white striped drum set. 

    2023: Kris Jenner’s Personalized Elves

    Kris Jenner showed off some elves of her own, revealing personalized Elf on the Shelf dolls for her 13 grandchildren — including Kourtney’s newborn son Rocky. 

    “It took me a few minutes to get this in order, but look what I have from Elf on the Shelf!” Kris said in her Instagram Story. “How cute is this?”

    “Mason, Penelope, North, Reign, Saint, Dream, Chicago, Stormi, True, Psalm, Aire, Tatum and baby Rocky!” the momager said as Mariah Carey’s “All I Want For Christmas Is You” played in the background.

    2023: Kylie Jenner’s Classic Tree

    The Kylie Cosmetics founder shared a sneak peek at her Christmas tree on Instagram Stories during Thanksgiving dinner.

    Kylie Jenner offered a close-up shot of the tree’s white lights and festive ornaments depicting snowmen, sleighs and Santa. 

    2022: Kourtney Kardashian's Crimson Christmas Trees

    The Poosh founder decided to create an indoor forest in her Calabasas home in 2022, featuring an array of sparkling red Christmas trees. Kourtney showed off the unique setup on her Instagram Story, revealing even more trees through the glass doors.

    2022: Kylie Jenner's Classic Christmas Tree

    Kylie opted for a classic look again in 2022 after years of unconventional hues, eye-popping ornaments and faux-snow-covered branches. (She first made the switch in 2020.)

    "Tis the season," the beauty mogul wrote alongside a video documenting her Christmas tree decorating process on Instagram.

    In a few clips, Kylie revealed a heaping pile of shiny ornaments that she used to decorate her ceiling-skimming evergreen. Michael Bublé's holiday hit, "It's Beginning to Look a Lot Like Christmas," played in the background as two younger members of the Kardashian-Jenner clan admired the twinkling tree.

    2022: Kim Kardashian's Bathroom Forest

    For the SKIMS founder, the more Christmas trees, the merrier! Kim couldn't help but show off her bathroom's "magical" tree display on her Instagram Story.

    "In my bathroom, I always have plants out there, and there's like a whole balcony and you can go through my shower out there, but right now this is all I see and it's so beautiful," the reality star said in the clip.

    2022: Kendall Jenner's Christmas Tree

    Kendall Jenner shared her towering Christmas tree, decorated with golden lights and silver tinsel, that she set up in the corner of her Los Angeles home. A coordinating lit evergreen garland decorates the mantel nearby.

    2022: Christmas Trees in the Kylie Cosmetics Office

    Kylie Jenner decided to spread holiday cheer in the office too! The makeup entrepreneur filled the headquarters with glowing metallic trees of different shapes and sizes.

    2022: Kris Jenner's Kitchen

    The momager got her kitchen ready for the holidays with some festive cookware and decorations. On her Instagram Story, Kris showed off her Santa Claus tea towels and plaid dutch ovens that adorned her stove, along with a delicate wreath hanging above.

    2022: Kim Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    Look familiar? The entrepreneur showed off the "snow-covered" tree in her living room which appeared very similar to her displays in recent years. Kim unveiled her tree on her Instagram Story and how she wakes her kids up during the holiday season.

    "It's that time of the year," she wrote on the video, adding that "@philthekeys comes over every morning to wake my kids up by playing the piano while the mad morning rush is happening to calm their little souls and fill it with beautiful Christmas songs."

    2020: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Tree

    Kylie's towering tree for 2020 was decidedly more classic than some of her past creations, though it was not subtle in size! "It's literally right at the ceiling," she said of the 18-plus-foot fir.

    The ornaments are a mix of vintage pieces her family had on their tree when she was younger and new ones in the same style — including fun food-related baubles depicting a coffee cup, a stack of chocolate chip cookies and a burger.

    "My mom used to put these on our tree every year, except I had to buy way more to fit this tree," Kylie explained with a laugh.

    2020: Kylie Jenner's Mantel

    In her living room, Kylie hung her embroidered vintage stockings by the chimney with care. "I had these made for you," Kris explained of the one bearing Kylie's name, which she's had since she was a little girl.

    The makeup mogul added a few more to her mantel, including for her daughter with Travis Scott, Stormi, as well as for Kris and Kris' longtime boyfriend Corey Gamble. The seated Santa in all-white is also a family heirloom. "I've had this since I was born," Kylie said of Old Saint Nick.

    2020: Stormi Webster's Christmas Tree

    Finally, Kylie shared a snapshot of a corner of Stormi's room, which she decked out with a miniature pink faux tree that sits in front of her daughter's picture book collection.

    2020: Kim Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    The SKKN by Kim founder seems to be following in mom Kris' holiday decorating footsteps and sticking with a handful of signature pieces she calls on each year.

    This classic evergreen is flocked (covered in fake snow) and has stood in this spot in the star's Hidden Hills home for the last several years. "Look how pretty," Kim noted in a video showing off the all-white scene.

    2020: Kim Kardashian's Hallway

    Another standout element filled with the spirit of Kardashian Christmases past is Kim's forest of fluffy trees. "If you didn't think I would bring back Whoville in the house," the mother of four said, referring to the fictional town from Dr. Seuss's How the Grinch Stole Christmas that many compared her fantastical sculptures when they made their debut at the family's 2018 Christmas Eve party.

    Kim's kids joined in the fun for an impromptu game of hide-and-seek amid the trees.

    2019: Kourtney Kardashian's Upside Down Tree

    An upside-down tree, complete with exposed roots, was the mom of three's unconventional choice for her Christmas tree in 2019.

    2019: Kourtney Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    The eldest Kardashian sister spent a "cozzzzzy morning" lounging on a massive shearling sofa in front of another twinkling tree. She's decked out in sister Kim's Skims clothing, naturally.

    2019: Kris Jenner's Stockings and Candles

    It's Kristmas time! The Kardashian-Jenner matriarch shared snapshots of her luxurious holiday decor to her Instagram Story featuring Chrissy Teigen and John Legend, who were invited over to her house for dinner. Teigen may have been on the naughty list, as Jenner caught her stealing out of the stockings!

    2019: Caitlyn Jenner's Christmas Tree

    "A very Christmasy Malibu sunset!" wrote the reality star, sharing a snap of her well-decorated evergreen in front of a gorgeous purple and pink sky.

    2019: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Tree

    The Kardashian-Jenner family has never been one to skimp on Christmas decorations, and in 2019, the youngest Jenner pulled out all the stops when it came to the festive fir decorating her front foyer.

    The makeup mogul shared a peek at the massive tree in her Hidden Hills home, which required a small team of people — at least four can be seen in the video — to help decorate and put up. The creative genius behind the snow-dusted, light-strung tree? Floral designer Jeff Leatham.

    2019: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Ornaments

    The young billionaire posed in front of her living room Christmas tree with family friend Fai Khadra before attending Sean "Diddy" Combs' 50th birthday bash. In a YouTube vlog, Jenner says she bought the same Christmas ornaments her mom, Kris, used to decorate her childhood tree with, using them to decorate her own.

    2019: Kylie Jenner's Office Christmas Tree

    The makeup mogul also decked out the Kylie Cosmetics office reception area with a neon pink Christmas tree trimmed with baby pink bulbs.

    2019: Kim Kardashian's Fluffy White Trees

    The former Keeping Up With the Kardashians star shared a series of photos she took in front of the monochromatic, sculptural trees adorning the hallways of her mansion. The all-white Christmas decor matches the home's minimalist aesthetic.

    "I love these. They're so whimsical, like Whoville, but all white," Kim said.

    The sculptures are the work of Australian contemporary artist Kathy Temin. An event designer familiar with her work estimated they came with "a price tag of at least $10,000 each."

    2019: Kim Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    Keeping with the spare, monochromatic white theme, Kim opted for a snow-covered faux fir to decorate her and then-husband Kanye West's home. "It's so pretty," she said while showing off the tree outfitted with white string lights in an Instagram video.

    2019: Khloé Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    The Khloé Kardashian's go-to designer, Jeff Leatham, strikes again! The Good American founder shared this snap of her pink, white and red Christmas tree, tagging Leatham in the caption. In the background, hanging stars can be seen illuminating the back terrace, above a dining table.

    2018: Kourtney Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    Kourtney turned her home into the North Pole in 2018 with five trees, a massive pile of presents and two polar bear statues (similar to her mom's!). She asked her followers to weigh in on the color scheme of her decor in an Instagram video, writing, "With our without the red?" in reference to the ball ornaments that decorated one of the three trees in her entryway.

    2018: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Tree

    For her first Christmas with baby Stormi, the new mom, then 21, went for solid gold. Her metallic tree is seemingly the same height as the previous year's holiday centerpiece: 20 feet!

    "Gold is the theme this year," Kylie captioned a video of the tree, with Mariah Carey's "All I Want for Christmas Is You" serving as the soundtrack. The towering conifer features gold branches, a variety of gold ball ornaments, countless glowing lights and even a gold-wrapped stand.

    2017: Kris Jenner's Christmas Trees

    In 2017, Kris opted for a rainbow-theme display and chose to feature four smaller trees as opposed to her previous peppermint-inspired giant. She also didn't limit herself to her entryway: Daughter Kim gave a tour of her mom's outdoor holiday scene, which included giant nutcrackers lining the path to the front door.

    2017: Kourtney Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    Kourtney's real Christmas secret weapon is surprisingly inexpensive, but she still amped it up in 2017 with giant gold ornaments and trees in each of her three kids' rooms, as well as her own.

    2017: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Tree

    The youngest KarJenner sibling may be the biggest fan of the holiday season. In 2017, she put up a 20-foot-tall snow-covered tree decked out in giant pink balls. "Thank you @jeffleatham & his team for making my XMAS dreams come true! 20 feet of magic! Even more perfect in real life … " she wrote in her Instagram caption.

    2017: Khloé Kardashian's Christmas Ornaments

    The reality star also shared a behind-the-scenes peek at the many bins of metallic ornaments she used to outfit her evergreens. "Decorating day," Khloé captioned a photo on Instagram, before revealing the glamorous final product in a glowing, close-up shot.

    2016: Kim Kardashian's Christmas Tree

    The mogul's best friend Jonathan Cheban was the one to reveal Kim's massive, monochromatic tree in 2016. "Oh my God Kim, this tree is huge," Cheban said in a snapchat video documenting the fabulous fir. "It's literally Rockefeller Center in Bel Air."

    2016: Kris Jenner's Christmas Tree

    Kris hosts an annual Christmas Eve party at her Calabasas, Calif. home and her decor has to live up to the hype of the star-studded fete. In 2016, Jenner called on Leatham to outfit the space in a "Kandyland chic" theme complete with peppermint-inspired ornaments on a massive tree and a life-size metal and glass polar bear guarding the staircase.

    "One of the fun things is just deciding on what the theme of the tree is going to be because that sets the tone for the entire house," Kris told Architectural Digest of the elaborate process.

    2016: Kendall Jenner's Christmas Tree

    To complement her very pink living room, model Kendall chose a comparatively modest, faux snow-covered tree. The white branches, created with a spray-on process called flocking, are popular with the whole family, who are guaranteed a green Christmas in Southern California.

    2016: Kourtney Kardashian's Christmas Trees

    Why have one Christmas tree when you can have six? The mom of three put up a small forest of evergreens in her home, including two in the soaring entry and one in each of her children's bedrooms.

    "Christmas morning takes place at my house and my entire family comes here, so I also have the responsibility to make it special for all of them, and I love doing it!" she explained in a post on her app.

    2016: Mason Disick's Christmas Tree

    Mason went traditional, decorating his tree with a snow globe and M-monogrammed ornaments, but added a personal touch with a super-sized figurine of Darth Vader standing guard nearby.

    2016: Penelope Disick's Christmas Tree

    Penelope went for a super feminine look, decorating her tree with purple and pink ball ornaments.

    2016: Reign Disick's Christmas Tree

    Reign even got his own pint-size tree decorated with Santas, animals and a snowman.

    2016: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Tree

    Kylie shared the elaborate — and at times nail-biting — process of loading her enormous flocked fir into the double-height entryway of her $6 million home in Hidden Hills.

    2016: Kylie Jenner's Gingerbread Houses

    The cosmetics maven also documented another extra Christmas installation in her home: a gingerbread mansion, decorated with the names of her then-boyfriend Tyga, his son King Cairo, and Kylie's beloved dogs Bambi, Norman, Ernesto and Penny.

    2015: Kris Jenner's Christmas Trees

    A glimpse back at her 2015 tree reveals the mom of 6 recycled her peppermint-themed ornaments in 2016. They make an equally over-the-top impact in their original installation, which includes two smaller trees flanking the star fir and a garland winding up the double staircases.

    2015: The Kardashian Family's Stockings

    Kendall snapped this fireside photo of the overwhelming number of stockings required by her growing family, which included embroidered beauties for matriarch Kris' boyfriend Corey Gamble, Caitlyn Jenner, Kourtney's ex Scott Disick, Kylie Jenner's on-and-off flame Tyga and newborn Saint.

    2015: Kourtney Kardashian's Christmas Trees

    The same year, Kourtney showed she didn't mess with a formula that was working its Christmas magic — the double trees in the entry from 2016, also turn up here.

    2015: Kourtney Kardashian's Gingerbread House

    Kourtney also revealed she may have been the originator of the gingerbread mansion trend. One year before Kylie's impressive construction, the eldest Kardashian sister showed off a cookie home with the caption, "Someone has been licking the house."

    2015: Kylie Jenner's Christmas Tree

    In her first home of her own, a then 18-year-old Kylie showed a glimpse of the Christmas maven she was born to become, sharing a snap of her fluffy tree outfitted with just a few metallic ornaments and some glitter.

    2014: Kris Jenner's Entryway

    Kris' holiday party likely set her girls' forthcoming fever in motion. Way back in 2014, Kendall posted a photo of her mom's impressive entry, including life-size nutcrackers and swags of greenery, all leading to her holiday centerpiece.

    2014: Kris Jenner's Christmas Tree

    Kris' 2014 tree was equally impressive sans polar bear, sporting a classic black-and-white theme that complimented her home's decor. Gold accents predicted the years of uber-glam Christmases to come.
