    KADY McDermott is the latest celebrity to be hacked by cyber pranksters

    The Love Island star fell victim to a online intruder and had her profile picture changed to an explicit image and name swapped to Heidy Jordan.

    A tweet was also sent which showed a pic of a nude bum with a thong barely covering a woman’s modesty with a link that led Kady’s followers through to a porn site.

    The reality star immediately deleted the offending tweet and told her fans that she had been “hacked”.

    The 20-year-old is the latest celeb to fall victim to a social media hacker.

    Last week Celebrity Big Brother star Chloe Khan had her entire Instagram account deleted by a hacker.

    The glamour girl took to Twitter to inform fans that her account was no more, she said:: “MY INSTAGRAM HAS BEEN HACKED AND DELETED AT 852K !!! #notleavingthissh*tillbeback”

    The reason for Chloe’s hack is yet unknown, although Love Island’s Katie Salmon may have shed some light, explaining that it also happened to her.
