    Early Life & Family

    Johnny Davis was born on February 23, 2002, in La Crosse, Wisconsin, United States, to Mark Giles Davis and Sarah Giles.

    Johnny’s father, Mark, is a former basketball player, and his mother, Sarah, is a homemaker. 

    The American basketball player has one older twin brother Jordan Davis and two younger twin siblings, Samantha Davis and Maxwell Davis. 

    His twin brother, Jordan, is also a basketball player who plays for Wisconsin.

    Father Mark Giles Davis

    Johnny’s father, Mark Giles Davis, is a former basketball player who played as a small forward in the NBA from 1985-1989.

    Mark was selected 79th overall in the 4th round by the Cleveland Cavaliers in the 1985 NBA Draft.

    While his time in NBA, he played for the Milwaukee Bucks (31 games) and Phoenix Suns (2 games), averaging 3.8 points and 1.1 rebounds per contest.

    While playing for CB Zaragoza, he won the Spanish Cup in 1990. He also participated in the 1991 European Cup Winner’s Cup final.

    High School & College

    Davis attended La Crosse Central School, where he received the Wisconsin Mr. Basketball title and the La Crosse Tribune Player of the Year award twice.

    Davis graduated with 2,158 career points, making him the school’s all-time leading scorer.

    Collegiate Career

    Despite getting offers from Minnesota, Iowa, and Marquette University, he enrolled at the University Of Wisconsin.

    In a 72-56 victory over Penn State on February 2, 2021, Davis recorded a rookie-season high of 17 points while going 6-of-7 from the field.

    He had a 19.7-point and 8.2 rebound average during his sophomore year and was named Player of the Year for the Big Ten.

    Davis forwent his remaining college eligibility on March 31, 2022, when he declared for the 2022 NBA Draft.

    NBA Career

    On June 23, 2022, NBA Draft, the Wizards selected Davis as the tenth overall pick.

    Davis was added to the Wizards’ NBA Summer League roster for 2022.

    In a 99-105 defeat against the Pistons, Davis scored six points on 1-for-11 shooting for the Summer League playing alongside Bradley Beal, Jordan Goodwin, Isaiah Todd, and Anthony Gill.

    Net Worth & Contracts

    Johnny Davis holds an estimated net worth between $2 million -$3 million as of December 2023. The majority of his earnings come from his basketball career and brand endorsements. 

    After being selected by the Wizards, Davis signed a four-year contract worth $21.20 million.

    His annual salary for the 2022-2023 season is $5.47 million.

    Similarly, for the upcoming 2023-2024 season, his annual salary will be $5.5 million.

    If he continues his NBA journey with the Wizards, after the 4-year rookie contract, he will become eligible for a $215 million rookie extension for five years with an annual salary of $37.07 million.

    Brand Endorsements

    Davis appeared in a sponsored endorsement for Taco Bell”s Toasted Cheddar Chalupa on May 24, 2022.

    Similarly, in 2022, the Wizards’ small forward, alongside his brother, Jordan, signed the endorsement deal with the Jockey.

    On December 15, 2022, he signed a contract with Adidas. He is also partnered with Muller Sports Medicine.
