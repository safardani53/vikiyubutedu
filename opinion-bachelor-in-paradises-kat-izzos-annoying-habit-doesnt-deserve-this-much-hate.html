    *WARNING: Spoilers ahead*

    Bachelor In Paradise is over halfway through its 10-episode season 9. With eight stars out of the luxurious Mexican location, things are getting heated and messy as love triangles begin to form. The latest romance complication came courtesy of Kat Izzo and Olivia Lewis, who were both vying for John Henry Spurlock’s heart. We all get nervous when it comes to dating but Kat’s habit is frustrating viewers more than expected.

    View Instagram Post

    Fans are ‘tired’ of Kat Izzo playing with her hair

    Whether it’s scratching or nail-biting, habits to cope with nervousness are pretty common, but when it’s broadcasted for the world to see, viewers will make an entire Reddit thread about it.

    One BIP fan was so distracted by Kat’s frequent hair-touching that they took to the platform in complaint.

    “Is anyone else tired of watching the women on BIP obsessively stroke their hair?” they posted.

    A user responded: “They are too self-aware of how they look on camera. I know that’s the way it is, but BIP would be more fun to watch if people were less camera-aware. The few who don’t make sure their facial expressions are on point are the most interesting to watch.”

    “She’s driving me mad doing this nonstop….arghhhh,” another added.

    View Tweet

    It could be a coping mechanism for nervousness

    According to Healthline, hair-twirling is a way of self-soothing to calm anxiety or nervousness. More severe cases can lead to trichotillomania, a mental health condition that causes the overwhelming urge to pull out one’s own hair.

    While it’s unknown exactly why Kat frequently touches her hair, the TV shouldn’t be teased for what could be a coping mechanism as a result of filming such a high-profile show.

    “I recognize it as a sign of an attempt to cope with anxiety,” a fan defended. “I don’t judge people for how they try to self-soothe.”

    “I’m just a normal woman and do the same thing with my hair when I’m nervous,” another chimed in.

    Spoilers – Kat Izzo rides off into the sunset with John Henry Spurlock

    It seems that Kat ended up on top in the Olivia and John Henry love triangle as Reality Steve revealed that she and underground welder John left paradise engaged.

    He reportedly popped the question in Mexico during the finale and are still together in November 2023. As expected, the stars have kept tight-lipped about their romance and have not posted anything about each other online. They follow each other on Instagram but that is expected for co-stars; John Henry also follows Olivia Lewis.
