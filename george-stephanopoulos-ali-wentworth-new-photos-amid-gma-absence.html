    ALI Wentworth has shared new photos while on vacation as her husband George Stephanopoulos continues to be absent from Good Morning America.

    George, 62, has been missing from the morning show all week.

    Whit Johnson has filled in for him every day so far.

    Most recently, the Good Morning America host's wife shared vacation photos from Greece that have not given any clue as to whether or not George is currently on the getaway.

    Ali uploaded two different Instagram posts that showed a collection of rocks and other colorful findings.

    She included the geotag of Spetses, Greece, and offered no caption on either of the social media posts.

    Ali's last post before the Greece shots showed her sitting down in front of a wheel of clay as she was making some sort of pottery.

    Fans took to the post's comment section to inquire about where her husband was.

    "I can imagine George just creeping up behind you to passionately kiss you from behind," one fan wrote, in reference to Ghost.

    One last fan asked: "Where’s George? Ghost movie replay."

    FAMILY VACATION?

    Last week, the ABC anchor was seen posing with his daughter, Harper, 18, for what seemed to be a vacation picture.

    It came after the host was praised by fans for having his daughter's prom outfit.

    In a carousel post made by Ali to celebrate their friend, Tracy Pollan's birthday, one photo had Ali, Tracy, George, Harper, and friends all smiling for a selfie on the beach.

    George and Harper were in the back, so only their heads were shown.

    When it comes to GMA anchors, George isn't the only one who has recently needed a fill-in.

    ROCKIN' ROBIN

    Yesterday, concerned fans addressed the fact that Robin Roberts, 62, was missing during GMA's Wednesday show after returning from her previous absences.

    ABC personality Rebecca Jarvis, 41, filled in for the TV personality during the June 28 show.

    Rebecca had to also step in for Robin on Monday due to her being out then as well.

    Robin did manage to muster up enough strength to come to work on Tuesday, revealing on Twitter that she was suffering from travel fatigue and fighting allergies.

    Included in her tweet was a clip of her looking camera ready, expressing her so-called Tuesday thoughts in her GMA dressing room.

    Fans poured in with their well-wishes on the social media platform.

    "Praying that you feel better Robin," one fan commented under her tweet.

    Another added: "Make sure & schedule a rest day. I encouraged my daughter to do this yesterday (she actually listened).

    "She has had 3 weeks of very busy work events & social events.

    "I so believe in the power of having a quiet mental day to recharge ourselves."

    A third person encouraged: "You've got this! Keep pushing through and shining bright with your allergies and travel fatigue are no match for your Tuesday motivation!"

    ROBIN RESPONDS

    Robin not only was absent from work this week, but also last week.

    Fill-in host Juju Chang, 57, replaced Robin for the morning show last Thursday.

    Robin explained in a tweet where she was instead of GMA on Thursday, thanking a fan named Beth, who tweeted about her being gone.

    "Glam fam & I traveling on assignment today," she wrote.

    "Heading to the heartland to do a story for next week. Thnx as always for looking out for our tribe!"

    However, Robin did not give specifics as to what she would be covering.
