    DUE to popular demand, McDonald's offers a discounted wrap every day of the week, giving customers the chance to get their hands on a tasty bargain.

    The wraps, which were only introduced by the fast-food chain in the 2000s, are now one of the favourite items on the McDonald's menu.

    What is McDonald's Wrap of the Day today?

    Fast-food chain McDonald's changes its menu of wraps daily, with different ones costing different amounts depending on the day.

    This gives customers the chance to add a little extra something tasty to their midday meal and save some cash.

    There are four different wrap flavours that are available on rotation at McDonald's.

    As of November 2023, these are The Caesar & Bacon Chicken One, The Sweet Chilli Chicken One, The Spicy Veggie One and The BBQ Chicken & Bacon One.

    Whether you have an allergy to a certain ingredient or just simply prefer your wrap without, you can customise your order to your liking.

    You can also add extras to your wrap of the day or make it a meal - but this will increase the cost a little, depending on what you choose to add.

    Here's everything you need to know about today's Wrap of the Day - and those you can treat yourself to throughout the week.

    Monday

    On a Monday, McDonald's customers have not one but two Wraps of the Day to choose from - including a veggie option.

    The first wrap of the day up for grabs is The Caesar & Bacon Chicken One.

    This menu item is made with crispy chicken breast, Caesar style sauce, crispy onions, bacon and lettuce in a soft, toasted tortilla wrap.

    Customers can also pick up The Spicy Veggie One at the start of the week.

    This vegan certified option is made with delicious veggie dippers coated in a spicy relish and topped off with crisp lettuce, red onion and tomato, all wrapped up in a soft, toasted tortilla wrap.

    Tuesday

    On Tuesday, the Wrap of the Day available is The BBQ and Bacon Chicken One.

    This wrap is made with grilled or crispy chicken, bacon, smoky BBQ sauce, cool mayo tomato and lettuce in a soft, toasted tortilla wrap.

    Wednesday

    As of November 2023, there is no Wrap of the Day listed for Wednesdays on McDonald's official website.

    Thursday

    On Thursday, customers can once again choose between two Wraps of the Day.

    The BBQ and Bacon Chicken One and The Spicy Veggie Wrap One are both on offer as part of the promotional deal.

    Friday

    On Friday, The Sweet Chilli Chicken One takes centre stage as Wrap of the Day.

    This wrap can be made with either grilled or crispy chicken - plus a tasty, sweet chilli sauce, cool mayo, lettuce and cucumber, all wrapped in a soft, toasted tortilla.

    Saturday

    As the weekend begins, McDonald's offer The Caesar & Bacon Chicken One as their Wrap of the Day.

    Sunday

    For the final day of the week, The Sweet Chilli Chicken One returns as the Wrap of the Day.

    How much do the McDonald's wraps cost?

    If you're purchasing a wrap of the day, then the good news is you will be saving yourself some money.

    As part of McDonald's promotional offers, the Wrap of the Day is cheaper to buy than the other Big Flavour wraps on the menu.

    All the wraps are the same price, no matter whether you go for grilled or crispy chicken.

    The Wrap of the Day costs £1.99.

    Each wrap outside of the promotional offer costs £3.49 on their own - or £4.99 as part of a meal - but prices can vary.

    Unfortunately for those wanting to enjoy McDonald's at home, the company confirmed in January 2023 that the £1.99 Wrap of the Day deal is no longer available on delivery.

    Those ordering the wraps through Uber Eats, Just Eat, or the My McDonald's app will need to pay full price.

    How many calories are in the McDonald's wraps?

    If you're wondering how many calories are in each of the McDonald's wraps - then we have everything you need to know.

    Here's the breakdown of calories in each wrap, according to McDonald's:

    • The Caesar & Bacon Chicken One - 532 kcal (crispy), 403 (grilled)
    • The Spicy Veggie One - 365 kcal
    • The Sweet Chilli Chicken One - 469 kcal (crispy), 320 (grilled)
    • The BBQ and Bacon Chicken One - 344 kcal

    Of course, if you are adding extras like fries or drinks to your meal then the calories count will change.

    A large coke adds another 290 kcals, while a large portion of McDonald's fries on the side is an extra 480 kcals. 

    Calorie guidelines are 2,000 a day for women and 2,500 for men.
