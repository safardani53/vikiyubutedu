    Vanessa Hudgens’ Boots & Shorts — SHOP If You Want To Rock The Over-The-Knee Look:

    ’70s style was all over the Fall 2015 runways at Fashion Week in NYC, and we couldn’t be happier that Vanessa is getting ahead of the trend and rocking the hot fall fashion look. With her pale, oversized sweater, tiny frayed denim shorts and super soft taupe over-the-knee moccasin boots, she’s definitely embracing the boho babe trend — and she did so in such great staples!

    The best part is that her exact shorts and boots are totally attainable– her ONEXONTEASPOON Wilde Bonita denim cut-off shorts are a great investment for the upcoming season at $99, and her ONEXONETEASPOON thigh-high taupe teaspoon moccasins are available for $348 (also in black).

    Looking for a less expensive option? Try these Chinese Laundry Riley 50/50 Over-the-Knee Stretch Boots, which are just $83. Whether you splurge or save you’ll snag Vanessa’s effortless style in no time!

    Vanessa Hudgens’ Denim Shorts & Thigh-High Boots In LA– Get The Look

    We love pairing short-shorts with thigh-high boots– showing that sliver of skin is undeniably sexy. However, if you’re like most of the country right now, you’re probably freezing your bottom off– but don’t worry! This trend can work for winter, too. Leave the denim shorts for summer, but try rocking a winter-appropriate pair of shorts in a thick wool or tweed. Then, just add a pair of cool tights, like these Vince Camuto sheer diamond patterned ones. The options are endless! A pair of opaque tights make the trend totally appropriate if you’re dealing with cooler temperatures, while the trend still looks chic sans tights if you work the right layers. Pair a button-down with a sweater and layer a coat over your shorts and boots combo and you are ready to go.

    We love these Joie Leander Shorts, and they’re on sale– bonus! Between sturdy shorts and cute stockings, you’ll be stylish and warm.

    Vanessa paired her shorts and boots with a cozy-looking sweater. That’s definitely a cold-weather staple, and one can never have enough sweaters– check out this BB Dakota Collective Stacey Hooded Cable Sweater for a comfy option. That’s one aspect of V’s outfit that we absolutely live in.

    Do you love the way Vanessa rocked this fierce boho babe trend?
