    BEN Shephard has announced 'very exciting' news about Tipping Point's next series.

    The TV presenter, 48, has been the face of the ITV game show since its debut almost 13 years ago.

    As the show continues to air on ITV1 every weekday at 4pm, Ben has dropped a big announcement.

    Taking to his social media, the Good Morning Britain anchor has announced a new series is on the way.

    And he's invited potential contestants to apply to take on the famous machine.

    In a video, he told his followers: "Hi there everybody, isn't it a beautiful day?"

    read more on tipping point

    He then introduced Ed, a member of the show's production team, before adding: "Very excited to tell you that we are planning to start filming a new series of Tipping Point, series 14 - but we need contestants.

    "I know lots of you always get excited when we do a new series and say 'how do we become contestants?'

    "Well, if you click on the link on this video then you can apply to be a contestant on the brand new series of Tipping Point."

    The dad-of-two revealed filming will kick off in the Spring.
