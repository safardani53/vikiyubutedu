    Away from the spotlight, and the well known 'Dogfather' is also happily married to wife Elinor Perry-Hall.

    Who is Graeme Hall's wife?

    Elinor Perry-Hall is a managing director of an investment firm called Pentlands, where she has been working for the last 20 years.

    She is the wife of Graeme Hall, a TV presenter and dog trainer who is based in rural Warwickshire.

    Originally from Painswick, Gloucestershire, Elinor and her husband share their pet dog, Lily, who often features on his Instagram.

    Elinor, who is a certified chartered accountant, also has her own Instagram page where she uploads pictures of her life with Graeme and their pooch, but her social media remains quite private.

    When did Graeme Hall get married?

    The happy couple got hitched in 2017, opting to hold the ceremony on a beach in Sri Lanka followed by a home celebration for family and friends at Mallory Court, a four-star country house, hotel and spa in Warwickshire.

    The pair have shared pictures of the happy day on their social media accounts since, with Graeme pictured sporting a pale blue checked waistcoat, a white shirt and a blue suit.

    Elinor is seen wearing a white gown with a plunging neckline with her brown locks swept up and out of her face, revealing dropper earrings.

    Other images from the day show that Elinor opted to change out of the gown and into a short, black, one-sleeved number so that she could dance the night away with her new husband.

    Does Graeme Hall have any children?

    Graeme does not have any children with Elinor, and has no kids from a previous relationship.

    It is not clear if his wife has any children, as she keeps her social media accounts private and does not share much of her private life with the public.

    But, she has on occasion referred to a daughter, notably in a tweet with a picture of the two women enjoying a spa day together with the hashtag #mumanddaughtertime.
