    03 Jul 2024, 00:00

    This week on VH1’s Love & Hip Hop Atlanta, the feuding Stevie J and Joseline met to discuss her pregnancy face to face for the first time.

    Meanwhile, Jasmine Washington showed Karlie Redd "proof" of Kirk Frost bonding with his alleged son … his secret alleged son.

    Did it get ugly AF in the ATL?

    Watch Love and Hip Hop: Atlanta Season 6 Episode 2 Online

    If you watch Love & Hip Hop Atlanta online, you know Joseline Hernandez and Stevie J have been at odds for quite some time now.

    Well, when they finally met face to face for the first time in months, she informed her longtime lover that she’ll be moving back to Florida.

    The mercurial Joseline was extremely upset that Stevie hasn’t taken a DNA test to prove whether or not he’s the father of her child.

    Stevie seemed to acknowledge thata point, telling her “I realize that if I take the test, it’s the only way we’re going to find out the truth.”

    Despite Joseline calling Stevie a deadbeat child molester, he reveals his feelings for her, and hints that their romance may not be over yet.

    With these two, you can’t rule anything out.

    Elsewhere on Love & Hip Hop Atlanta Season 6 Episode 2, Momma Dee and Ernest are dealing with real marital issues of their own.

    She also tells Lil Scrappy the “devastating news” that her mother-in-law is moving to Atlanta, which is going to be too close for comfort.

    As for fiancee Bambi Benson, he tells his mom that they’re currently separated, and he isn’t sure he “wants to get married, just to get divorced.”

    Note that Scrap is not actually married yet.

    While this is going on, Tommie Lee is out of jail and meeting up with her real mother, Samantha, and Scrapp Deleon’s mom Karen King.

    KK and Tommie have had their differences, to be sure, but Scrapp’s mom shares, “Even though we fight, she’s always going to be in my life.”

    In the storyline everyone’s been talking about, Karlie Redd meets up with Jasmine Washington to get the scoop on Jasmine’s love child.

    Karlie was not convinced about Jasmine’s claims that she’s been f–king Kirk Frost on the side, but Jasmine has a video to prove it.

    The video footage shows Kirk with her baby Kannon Mekhi Washington, strongly implying that he got his side chick pregnant all right.

    Jasmine says she knows that Kirk was married to Rasheeda, obviously, since he would bring their son Karter over to her house sometimes.

    He also moved her into their old building, downstairs, so that they could be closer together, and got a second phone to talk to her on.

    Yeah. This is all quite a lot to process right here.

    Oh, did we mention that Jasmine has a boyfriend?

    Rodney Bullock is also dealing with the fallout from Jasmine’s baby, as he describes to Young Joc in a conversation between friends.

    “Kirk has been sliding her bread here and there paying the bills,” Rodney reveals to his buddy, but the big bombshell is yet to come.

    Karlie outs Jasmine to Rasheeda, Kirk’s wife.

    Recounting their 17 years together, and the gravity of what Karlie just told her, Rasheeda can’t keep it together as tears start flowing.

    "This s–t is hurtful as f–k,” Rasheeda says.

    Confronting Kirk, she blatantly asks about Jasmine’s video, questioning “what the f–k is up" with it, as we leave off on that cliffhanger.
