    Bio:
    Brittany Snow (born Brittany Anne Snow on March 9, 1986 in Tampa, Florida) is an American actress and singer. Brittany began her career in show business at the age of three, appearing in a print ad for the Burdines department store. From 1998 to 2002, she portrayed Susan “Daisy” Lemay on the daytime soap opera, Guiding Light. She continued her television success with being cast as Meg Pryor on the NBC drama, American Dreams. In 2005, she made her film debut in the Vin Diesel movie, The Pacifier. Afterwards, she appeared in movies such as John Tucker Must Die, the remake of Hairspray and the 2008 remake of the 1980 horror film, Prom Night. She has also appeared in television shows and films like Harry’s Law, Gossip Girl, Ben and Kate, and 96 Minutes. In 2012, Brittany portrayed Chloe Beale in Pitch Perfect. She reprised the role in the 2015 sequel, Pitch Perfect 2.

    Best Known For:
    Brittany is best known as an actress, including the role of Chloe Beale in the Pitch Perfect film series.

    Personal Life:
    Brittany has spoken openly about her battles with eating disorders, depression and self-harm. She started the Love is Louder movement with the Jed Foundation in 2010, offering support to anyone feeling mistreated or alone. She voiced the character Namine in the video game, Kingdom Hearts II. She has appeared in multiple music videos for bands Otep, Plushgun, Ashley Tisdale and LP. Her mother, Cinda Snow, is the founder and CEO of Snow Entertainment. She has been in a relationship with Teen Wolf actor Tyler Hoechlin since 2013.
