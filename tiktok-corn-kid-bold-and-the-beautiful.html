    TARIQ LOGAN made headlines in 2022 after his interview about corn went viral.

    Tariq, who was dubbed the "Corn Kid," has since capitalized on the fame and even starred in a few episodes of The Bold and the Beautiful.

    When was Tiktok's Corn Kid on The Bold and the Beautiful?

    Since going viral in 2022, Logan has been seen at red-carpet events, in Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade, on The Drew Barrymore Show, and most recently, in multiple episodes of The Bold and the Beautiful.

    He made his acting debut on the show in May 2023 portraying the role of Orville, the grandson of Jimmie Walker's “Count Bouche” character, in episodes that ran on May 3 and May 4.

    “I was really nervous, but I loved acting,” Logan said of his soap opera appearance, via The Blast. “I cannot wait to do it again.”

    That week he was among three celebrity guest stars, including Walker and Italian Celebrity Chef Daniele Uditi.

    Who is Tariq Logan?

    Logan is a seven-year-old boy from New York City known for his love of corn.

    He first went viral in August 2022 after appearing in an interview with Recess Therapy.

    In the clip, he talked about his love for corn, saying: "I love corn...I mean look at this thing, I can't imagine a more beautiful thing."

    Shortly after the clip went viral, TikTok user @schmoyoho turned the interview into a song, which went viral on the social media site and received over 31million views on YouTube.

    How can I watch The Bold and the Beautiful?

    The Bold and the Beautiful is CBS' long-running soap opera series that focuses on the wealthy and powerful Forrester family.

    The show first premiered in 1987 and has since been on the air for over 35 seasons.

    New episodes of the show can be watched live on CBS on weekdays, starting at 1.30pm EST.

    The episodes can also be streamed on Paramount+ with an active subscription.
