    JESSICA Simpson has revealed a long-ago "secret romance" with a mystery "Movie Star" as many fans guess that the mystery man is actor Mark Wahlberg.

    The With You singer opened up about their coincidental run-ins and planned rendezvous during the early 2000s in her short, but true, story, Movie Star: They Always Say They're Single.

    While promoting the book on her Instagram, Jessica, 42, asked her fans: "Want to hear a pretty wild story?

    She continued: "Well, I found some words that were locked away in a secret journal, one I never thought I’d open again."

    Within its opening pages, the Dukes of Hazzard actress revealed what her life as a music pop star was like in 2001.

    Jessica disclosed that she and her longtime boyfriend, and eventually husband, Nick Lachey had recently broken up and that she had two boyband members, one from the Backstreet Boys and the other from *NSYNC, hot on her heels.

    She recounted taking a photo with Jennifer Lopez at an MTV VMAs after-party and how rapper "Ja Rule photobombed" the snapshot.

    The Where You Are artist also talked about feeling insecure as she walked the red carpet, that is until she had a run-in with a certain, "massive movie star."

    Jessica withheld the actor's name, simply calling him "Movie Star," throughout the text, but she did go on to discuss their private romance that ignited that night.

    She revealed that upon meeting the Movie Star the pair shared a hug or as she explained, "a modified celebrity embrace he held a few seconds longer" than she expected.

    Jessica then touched upon several missed opportunities between her and the famous actor and how after her marriage to the 98 Degrees frontman had ended the pair would reconnect.

    The 42-year-old talked about meeting the Movie Star at the Beverly Hills Hotel before an award show, kissing him, and later seeing the actor with his girlfriend on the red carpet.

    A third commenter added: "100% Marky Mark, especially with the posters on the wall comment."

    DATING HISTORY

    Jessica has been involved in relationships with both famous musicians and popular athletes.

    Following her divorce from Nick Lachey, Jessica began dating singer John Mayer.

    The two first met in January 2005 at a GRAMMY Awards party held by Clive Davis.

    Fast forward to the next year, the pair struck up a romance during the summer of 2006.

    Jessica and John broke up a total of nine times before calling it quits for good sometime in June 2007.

    While recalling her relationship with John, Jessica mentioned he "was obsessed with me, sexually, and emotionally" in her memoir Open Book.

    "I would get up to go to the bathroom, and John would ask, 'Where are you going?'

    "While I was married, my ex-husband couldn't be bothered to figure out what city I was in.

    "It felt safe to be desired. I know John would never cheat on me, that confidence was a new feeling for me."

    She further elaborated: "He'd dump me, then come back saying he had discovered he loved me after all.

    "I always saw it as him mercifully taking me in from the cold.

    "Every time John returned, I thought it was a continuation of a love story, while my friends saw a guy coming back for sex with some foolish girl.

    "To think that every single time I lay on the ground, broken and bewildered, he took his time walking over. Observing me to jot down notes and hum a new song of heartbreak."

    During an appearance on The TODAY Show, Jessica explained to Hoda Kotb: "The relationship was very complex. It was always on again, off again, on again, off again, and I went back close to 9 times.

    "We were great at intimacy. We were great at loving each other. That was easy."

    By November 2007, Jessica moved on with Dallas Cowboys quarterback Tony Romo.

    On July 9, 2009, Romo and the Billboard Award nominee hit splitsville when he discovered texts from John in her phone.

    Coincidentally, the NFL player broke up with her the day before her 29th birthday.

    DIVORCE AND MARRIAGE

    On November 23, 2005, Jessica and her then-husband Nick Lachey announced they were proceeding with a divorce.

    On June 20, 2006, the former spouses were officially divorced.

    Regarding her first marriage, Jessica confessed in her memoir: "Nick and I were very great at being together publicly and on-camera.

    "We were best at our relationship when we were singing together. I felt like we were at home in that place.

    "But as far as doing the reality show, we just kind of had fun with it until the end — until we started having marital problems.

    "I don't believe that the show is what tore our marriage apart."

    On July 5, 2014, Jessica tied the knot with her second husband Eric Johnson.

    The singer and the NFL tight end entered a relationship in May 2010.

    The wedded pair are parents to three children, two daughters and one son.
