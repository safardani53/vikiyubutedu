    Popular 5 Fashion Trends Started In Korea By BLACKPINKs Jennie

    29 May 2024, 00:00

    BLACKPINK’s Jennie has always made headlines for the way she handles herself. Many people admire her sense of style and fashion. This is also why she is a brand ambassador for a slew of high-end labels, like Chanel, Calvin Klein, and Gente Monster, to name a few.

    From bleached bangs to graphic eyeliners, Jennie has experimented with and promoted a variety of styles, particularly in Korea. When someone is spotted wearing anything, it quickly becomes a popular subject of discussion among netizens on social media.

    BLACKPINK’s Jennie launched popular trends.

    1) Hair clips with glitter

    Jennie’s hairpins, which she wore throughout her SOLO phases, we’re trending on Naver, igniting a new trend in Korea. She matched her hair item to each of her ensembles, which quickly became a worldwide trend. Almost every accessory business stocked these sparkly clips, now known as Jennie clips.

    2) Tops with crescent moon prints

    She originally wore these outfits in the teaser for BLACKPINK’s song Ddu-du Ddu-du, and she also wore them throughout the return stages for the same. The spectators were drawn in by the body-hugging naked crescent moon top. To many people’s amazement, Jennie created an item of apparel that seemed silly and plain.

    3) Bow brooch by Gucci

    During her MMA 2016 debut, Jennie made a statement in a schoolgirl plaid costume accented with a Gucci bow brooch. Korean netizens began searching for similar knock-offs under the hashtag Jennie brooch. Many other female groups were also spotted sporting clothes similar to the one Jennie popularized.

    4) Tweed attire

    Jennie adores Chanel, a premium label famed for its tweed apparel. Jennie is a Chanel brand ambassador, and she was recently observed wearing a variety of tweed ensembles, bolstering the brand’s appeal in Korea. Because of her influence, tweed jacket sales have more than doubled.

    5) Cardigans with half-button closures

    Jennie adores cardigans and is seldom seen without one. She normally wears it by buttoning just the middle fastening and leaving the rest of the cardigan unbuttoned. This look immediately gained popularity as more and more celebrities began wearing their cardigans in the same manner. The trend may seem simple, but it gives a pop of color to a plain cardigan.

    Nobody can dispute that BLACKPINK’s Jennie is a trailblazer. Her popular styles have made the rounds to date, with more and more individuals finding them and jumping on the Jennie fashion bandwagon.
