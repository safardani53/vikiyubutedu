    Pete Sampras, an American former world No. 1 tennis player, has three siblings, Stella, Marion, and Gus Sampras.

    Among the Sampras siblings, Pete is the most famous one, as he was a former professional tennis player.

    Likewise, He first reached the world’s No. 1 ranking in 1993 and held that position for 286 weeks from 1993 to 1998.

    In addition, he was also inducted into the prestigious International Tennis Hall of Fame.

    Pete Sampras Sister Marion And Stella: Brother Gus

    Petros Sampras was born on August 12, 1971, in Washington, D.C, to his parents, Sam and Georgia Sampras.

    His mother emigrated from Sparta, Greece, and his father was born in the United States.

    He grew up with his three siblings, Stella, Marion, and Gus Sampras, among which Stella is the eldest, a women’s tennis head coach at UCLA and a former professional tennis player.

    Formerly she played tennis for the UCLA Bruins from 1987 to 1991 and went to Palos Verdes High School.

    Stella married a marketing executive Steve Webster on Jan. 3, 2003.

    Later in 2005, the couple was blessed with twin daughters, Sophia and Savannah.

    The twins also have a pet dog of Morkie (half Maltese and half Yorkshire) breed named Simba, who loves to spend time with their paternal grandmother, Mary C Webster.

    Likewise, Gus, his brother, is the second child who worked as the Regional Events Supervisor at In-N-Out Burger.

    Pete is the middle child and then comes the youngest, Marion, a teacher in Los Angeles.

    Dating Timeline Of Pete: Kids

    Before Pete tied the knot with his wife, Bridgette Wilson, in 2000, he was dating Kimberly Williams-Paisley (1997 – 1999).

    Later, the couple was blessed with two beautiful kids, Christian Charles Nikolaos, born in 2002, and Ryan in 2005.

    His wife, Bridgette Wilson, was born on September 25, 1973, in Gold Beach, Oregon, United States.

    Bridgette is a famous actress, singer, former Miss Teen USA, and model. Her most recent work was Mortal Kombat 11 (2020) in a Sonya Blade role.

    The couple’s first meet was co-incident as Sampras ran into her at a Los Angeles movie theatre.

    Likewise, in In-Style Magazine, Pete explained, “I replied to my friend, half-jokingly, “I’d love to see her,” and the friend set up their meeting.”

    Shared Business Venture With Gus Sampras

    Tennis legend Pete Sampras, holder of a remarkable 14 Grand Slam titles, established Pure Sports Management, LLC in 2006.

    This California-based management company partner with his brother Gus, an experienced sports marketing executive.

    The company aims to overseas Sampras’ business ventures and manages other athletes.

    Gus Sampras implied his 18 years of sports marketing experience, having previously served as a vice president at IMG Tennis.
