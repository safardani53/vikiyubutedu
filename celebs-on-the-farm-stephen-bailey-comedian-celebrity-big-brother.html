    STEPHEN Bailey is known as a UK stand-up comedian and TV presenter.

    He has presented Channel 5’s Celebs On The Farm since 2018 - let's take a look at Stephen's life and career so far.

    Who is Stephen Bailey?

    Stephen is a professional comedian and a veteran of the Edinburgh Festival.

    He was born in Manchester and made a name for himself on the comedy circuit before being snapped up by TV and radio bosses who saw his star quality.

    Stephen, who was named as one of Attitude Magazine's Top 100 Bachelors of the Year in 2017, has supported Katherine Ryan and Jenny Eclair on their UK tours.

    Stephen has worked as the resident agony aunt on on Zoe Ball on Saturday/Sunday.

    As well as his TV career, Stephen has hosted Virgin Radio’s Sunday Takeover, The Sunday Sessions with Laura Whitmore on Radio 5 Live and on Absolute’s The Jason Manford Show.

    What TV shows has Stephen been in?

    Stephen has hosted Channel 5’s Celebs On The Farm since 2018, as well as Celebs on the Ranch.

    He has also featured on Big Brother’s Bit On The Side and has also appeared on Sunday Morning Live, It's Not Me, It's You , Safeword and CelebAbility.

    Who is Rich Taylor and what is he known for?

    Rich Taylor is Stephen's partner who is an employment lawyer.

    The pair are set to appear in BBC One's Unbreakable where they will test their relationship alongside other celebrity couples.
