    Brandy Ends Engagement to Ryan Press

    Its all over for Brandy Norwood and Ryan Press. The singer and her music executive boyfriend announced their engagement in late 2012, but Brandys rep now says her client needs to find herself before settling down.

    02 Aug 2024, 00:00

    It’s all over for Brandy Norwood and Ryan Press.

    A rep for the artist has confirmed that she and her fiancé have ended their engagement, but “the friendship is still on.”

    The singer and her music executive boyfriend announced their engagement in late 2012, but Brandy’s rep now says her client needs to find herself before settling down.

    “They decided to take a step back and reevaluate the relationship. Brandy is 35 now — that’s what her whole #35 on Twitter is about, becoming her own woman.”

     

    Brandy has a 12-year old daughter named Sy’rai with ex Robert Smith, but she has never been tied the knot.

    “Brandy has a daughter and she still wants to be married and have more kids but she is working on so many other things right now,” the rep adds, saying of her and Press:

    “They haven’t ended things on a bad note and they still speak all the time.”

    Brandy, of course, is the sister of Ray J, best known for manning the camera of the Kim Kardashian sex tape.
