    Where was What About Bob filmed?

    What About Bob? is a 1991 comedy film directed by Frank Oz.

    It stars Bill Murray, as Bob Wiley, and Richard Dreyfuss, as Dr Leo Marvin.

    Bob is a troubled patient who follows his self-centered psychotherapist Dr Leo Marvin on vacation.

    According to the Boston Globe, What About Bob? was filmed at Smith Mountain Lake, Virginia, not Lake Winnipesaukee, New Hampshire, as referenced in the movie.

    Where can I watch What About Bob?

    What About Bob? is available to watch for free on tubi, fuboTV, or Philo, as long as you have a subscription to one of the three streaming platforms.

    The film can be rented, however, for $3.99 on YouTube, Apple TV, Amazon Prime Video, Vudu and Google Play.

    It is currently unavailable on Netflix and Hulu.
